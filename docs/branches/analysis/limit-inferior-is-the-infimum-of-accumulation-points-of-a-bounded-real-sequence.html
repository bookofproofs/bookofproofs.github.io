Proposition: Limit Inferior is the Infimum of Accumulation Points of a Bounded Real Sequence

Let $H$ be the non-empty1 set of all accumulation points of a bounded real sequence $(a_n)_{n\in\mathbb N}.$ The limit inferior of $a_n$ equals the infimum of $H$, formally $$\underline{\lim_{n\to\infty} a_n}=\inf H.$$
