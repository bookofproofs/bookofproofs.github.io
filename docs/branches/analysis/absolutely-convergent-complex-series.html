Definition: Absolutely Convergent Complex Series

A complex series \(\sum_{k=0}^\infty x_k\) is a absolutely convergent complex series, if the series of absolute values of the summands \[\sum_{k=0}^\infty |x_k|\] is a convergent complex series.
