Proposition: \(\exp(0)=1\)

For any convergent real sequence \((x_n)_{n\in\mathbb N}\) with \(\lim_{n\to\infty} x_n=0\) we have \[\lim_{x\to\infty}\exp(x_n)=1.\] In other words \(\lim_{x\to 0}\exp(x)=1\) or \[\exp(0)=1.\]
