Proposition: Algebraic Structure of Complex Numbers Together with Addition and Multiplication

The set of complex numbers \(\mathbb C\), together with the specific addition operation "\(+\)", and the specific multiplication operation "\(\cdot\)", forms the algebraic structure \((\mathbb C, + , \cdot)\) of a field.
