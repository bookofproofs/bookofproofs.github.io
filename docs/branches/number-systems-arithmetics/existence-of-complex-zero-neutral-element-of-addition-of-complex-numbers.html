Proposition: Existence of Complex Zero (Neutral Element of Addition of Complex Numbers)

There exists a complex number \(0\in\mathbb C\) such that \[x+0=0+x=0\] for all \(x\in\mathbb C\), i.e. \(0\) is neutral with respect to the addition or complex numbers.
