Proposition: Existence of Real Zero (Neutral Element of Addition of Real Numbers)

There exists a real number \(0\in\mathbb R\) such that \[x+0=0+x=x\] for all \(x\in\mathbb R\), i.e. \(0\) is neutral with respect to the addition or real numbers.
