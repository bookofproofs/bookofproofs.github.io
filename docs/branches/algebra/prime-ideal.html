Definition: Prime Ideal

An ideal $I\lhd R$ in a commutative ring $(R, + ,\cdot)$ is called a prime ideal, if

  • $I\neq R$ and
  • for any $r,s\in R$ with $rs\in I$ it follows $r\in I$ or $s\in I.$
