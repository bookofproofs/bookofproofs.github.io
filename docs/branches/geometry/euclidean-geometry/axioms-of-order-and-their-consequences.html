Section: Axioms of Order and Their Consequences

The next group of axioms comprises rules governing the between" relation of "points that lie on a straight line and allows ordering points on a line. Hilbert did not invent these axioms by himself but used the work of "Moritz Pasch":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/moritz-pasch (1843 - 1930), who also worked on the foundations of geometry before him.
