Definition: 7.02: Number

And a number (is) a multitude composed of units.

Modern Definition

For modern definitions of numbers see number systems and onwards.

The above means the result of the addition $n$ times, $n > 1$ the unit to itself:

\[n=\underbrace{1+\ldots+1}_{n\text{ times}}.\]

Notes

  • Euclid uses in his "Elements" also another notion of a number - a magnitude, which means a positive real number.
  • Neither negative numbers nor the number \(0\) are defined in Euclid's “Elements”.
