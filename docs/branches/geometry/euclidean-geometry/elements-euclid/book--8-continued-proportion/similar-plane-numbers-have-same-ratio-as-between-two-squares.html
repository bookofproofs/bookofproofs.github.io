Proposition: Prop. 8.26: Similar Plane Numbers have Same Ratio as between Two Squares

Euclid's Formulation

Similar plane numbers have to one another the ratio which (some) square number (has) to a(nother) square number. * Let $A$ and $B$ be similar plane numbers. * I say that $A$ has to $B$ the ratio which (some) square number (has) to a(nother) square number.
