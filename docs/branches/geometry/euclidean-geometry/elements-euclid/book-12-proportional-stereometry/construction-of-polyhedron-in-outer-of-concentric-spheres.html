Proposition: Prop. 12.17: Construction of Polyhedron in Outer of Concentric Spheres

Euclid's Formulation

There being two spheres about the same center, to inscribe a polyhedral solid in the greater sphere, not touching the lesser sphere on its surface. * Let two spheres have been conceived about the same center, $A$. * So, it is necessary to inscribe a polyhedral solid in the greater sphere, not touching the lesser sphere on its surface.
