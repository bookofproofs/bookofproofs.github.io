Proposition: Prop. 10.012: Commensurability is Transitive Relation

Euclid's Formulation

(Magnitudes) commensurable with the same magnitude are also commensurable with one another. * For let $A$ and $B$ each be commensurable with $C$. * I say that $A$ is also commensurable with $B$.
