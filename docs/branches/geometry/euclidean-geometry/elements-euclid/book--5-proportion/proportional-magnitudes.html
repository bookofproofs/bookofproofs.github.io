Definition: 5.06: Proportional Magnitudes

And let magnitudes having the same ratio be called proportional.

Modern Formulation

Iif the positive real numbers $\alpha$ and $\beta$ have the same ratio as $\gamma$ and $\delta$ then they are proportional, formally

\[\frac\alpha\beta=\frac\gamma\delta.\]
