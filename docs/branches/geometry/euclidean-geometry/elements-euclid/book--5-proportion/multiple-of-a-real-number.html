Definition: 5.02: Multiple of a Real Number

And the greater (magnitude is) a multiple of the lesser when it is measured by the lesser.

Modern Formulation

A positive real number \(\beta > 0\) is called a multiple of another positive real number1 \(\alpha\), if there exists a natural number \(k > 1\) such that[^2] \[\beta=k\cdot \alpha.\]
