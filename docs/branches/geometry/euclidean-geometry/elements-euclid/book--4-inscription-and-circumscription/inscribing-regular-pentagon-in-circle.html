Proposition: 4.11: Inscribing Regular Pentagon in Circle

Euclid's Formulation

To inscribe an equilateral and equiangular pentagon in a given circle. * Let $ABCDE$ be the given circle. * So it is required to inscribe an equilateral and equiangular pentagon in circle $ABCDE$.

Modern Formulation

It is possible to inscribe a regular pentagon in a given circle.
