Definition: Relation

Let \(S_1, \ldots, S_n\) be (not necessarily different), $n\ge 1$, sets. Any subset of the Cartesian product \(R\subseteq S_1\times \ldots\times S_n\) is called an $n$-ary relation or just a relation.
