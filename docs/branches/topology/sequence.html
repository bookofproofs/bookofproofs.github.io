Definition: Sequence

Let \((X)\) be a (non-empty) set. A sequence is a function $f:M\to X$ from a subset $M\subseteq \mathbb Z$ of integers to \(X\). We denote a sequence of points \(a_n\in X\) as \((a_n)_{n\in M}.\)
