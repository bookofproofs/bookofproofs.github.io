Proof

(related to Algorithm: Extended Greatest Common Divisor (Python))

  • By hypothesis, \(a,b\in\mathbb{Z}\) are positive integers.
  • The existence of solutions of an LDE with two variables ensures the existence of a solution.
  • In the algorithm, the greatest common divisor (python) algorithm has been extended by auxiliary variables $x,y,u,v$ are used for calculating the coefficients $a,b$ by backward substitution.
  • The runtime is the same as in the original algorithm.
