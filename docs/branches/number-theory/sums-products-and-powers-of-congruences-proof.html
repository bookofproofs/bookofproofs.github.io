Proof

(related to Corollary: Sums, Products, and Powers Of Congruences)

  • The sum and product formulae follow by induction from the proposition about addition, subtraction and multiplication of congruences1.

  • The power formula follows from the product formula by setting $a:=a_1=\ldots=a_r.$
