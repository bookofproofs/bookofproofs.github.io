Definition: Even and Odd Numbers

An integer $n$ is called even, if it is divisible by $2$ (i.e. $2\mid n$), otherwise (i.e. if $2\not\mid n$) it is called odd.

Examples

  • $0,2,10,-20,250,\ldots$ are even.
  • $1,-1,3,-3,255,-33,\ldots$ are odd.
