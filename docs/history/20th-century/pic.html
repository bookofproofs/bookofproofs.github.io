Person: Pic, Gheorghe

Gheorghe Pic was a Czech mathematician who worked in algebra.

Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):

  1. Founded the Institute for Mathematics and Physical Sciences in Romania
  2. Wrote over 40 papers on analytic function theory and harmonic analysis
  3. Developed an innovative approach to the problem of proving theorems of analytic function theory based on the notion of a Cauchy integral
  4. Pioneered the study of multiple Fourier series and their applications
  5. Devised a fundamental theorem for the existence of analytic functions
  6. Established a theory for the expansion of functions in series
  7. Pioneered the understanding of the Riemann surface and its importance in complex analysis
  8. Was a key mentor of influential Romanian mathematician Grigore Moisil

Born 18 March 1907, Czakova ?, Upper Silesia (now Czech Republic). Died 23 September 1984, Cluj, Romania.
