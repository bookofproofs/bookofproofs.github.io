Let `\(\phi,\psi:[a,b]\mapsto\mathbb R\)` be [step functions over the closed interval][bookofproofs$1751] `\([a,b]\)`. The **order relation for step functions** is defined as 
`\[\phi\le \psi:\Longleftrightarrow \phi(x)\le \psi(x)\quad\text{for all }x\in[a,b].\]`
