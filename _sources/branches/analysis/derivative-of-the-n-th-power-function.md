The [derivative][bookofproofs$1370] of the [$n$-th power function][bookofproofs$1618] `\(f(x)=x^n\)`, `\(x\in\mathbb R\)`, `$n\in\mathbb N$`, is `\[f'(x)=nx^{n-1}.\]`
