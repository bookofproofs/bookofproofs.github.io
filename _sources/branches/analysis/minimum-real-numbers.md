Let `\(D\)` be a [non-empty subset][bookofproofs$552] of [real numbers][bookofproofs$1105]. The real number `\(M\)`, denoted by `\(\min(D)\)`, is called the *minimum of `$D$`* if and only if

* `$M$` is a [infimum][bookofproofs$1755] of `$D$`, and
* `$M\in D$`.
