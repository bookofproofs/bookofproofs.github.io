For any [positive real number][bookofproofs$1107] `\(a > 0\)`, the [limit][bookofproofs$141] of [n-th roots][bookofproofs$46] equals `\(1\)`, formally

`\[\lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]a=1.\]`
