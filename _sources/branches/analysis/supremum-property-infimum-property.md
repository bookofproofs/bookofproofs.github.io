Every [non-empty subset][bookofproofs$552] of [real numbers][bookofproofs$1105], which has [an upper bound][bookofproofs$584], has also a [supremum][bookofproofs$1754]. Equivalently, we say that real numbers have the **supremum property**.

Every [non-empty subset][bookofproofs$552] of [real numbers][bookofproofs$1105], which has [a lower bound][bookofproofs$584], has also an [infimum][bookofproofs$1754]. Equivalently, we say that real numbers have the **infimum property**.
