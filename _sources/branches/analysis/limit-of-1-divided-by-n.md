Let `$(x_n)_{n\in\mathbb N}$` be a [real sequence][bookofproofs$875] with `$$x_n:=\frac 1n,\quad\quad n\in\mathbb N,~n > 0.$$`
Then  `$(x_n)_{n\in\mathbb N}$` is [convergent][bookofproofs$141] with
`$$\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac 1n=0.$$`
