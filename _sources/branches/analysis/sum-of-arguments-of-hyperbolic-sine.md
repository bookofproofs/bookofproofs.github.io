The following formula holds for the calculation of the [sum][bookofproofs$261] of arguments of the [hyperbolic sine][bookofproofs$6688] `$\sinh$`: `$$\sinh(x+y)=\cosh(x)\sinh(y)+\sinh(x)\cosh(y)$$`
for all `$x\in\mathbb R,$` where `$\cosh$` denotes the [hyperbolic cosine][bookofproofs$6687].
