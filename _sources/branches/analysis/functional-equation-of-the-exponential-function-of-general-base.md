For all real `\(x,y\in\mathbb R\)`, and all real `\(a > 0\)`, the [exponential function of general base][bookofproofs$1603] `\(a  > 0 \)` fulfills the following functional equation

`\[\exp_a(x+y)=\exp_a(x)\cdot \exp_a(x).\]`
