For any [real numbers][bookofproofs$1105] `\(x,y\in\mathbb R\)`, the following **reverse triangle inequalities** hold:

1. `$|x+y|\ge |x|-|y|,$`
1. `$|x+y|\ge |x|-|y|,$`
