Let `$x_1,\ldots,x_n$` be [non-negative real numbers][bookofproofs$1107] for some [natural number][bookofproofs$718] `$n\in\mathbb N.$` Then the following inequality holds:

`$$\prod_{k=1}^n(1+x_k)\ge 1+\sum_{k=1}^n x_k.$$`
