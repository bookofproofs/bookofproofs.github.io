Let `$0 < a < b$`. The [Riemann-integral][bookofproofs$1370] of the [natural logarithm][bookofproofs$1421] on the [closed real interval][bookofproofs$1153] is given by the formula

`$$\int_a^b\log(x)dx= x(\log(x)-1)\;\begin{array}{|l}a\\\\b\end{array}.$$`
