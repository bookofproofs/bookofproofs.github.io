Let `$f,g:D\to\mathbb R$` be `$n$` times differentiable. The [$n$-th derivative][bookofproofs$6771] of the product `$fg$` is given by 

`$$\frac{d^kf(x)g(x)}{dx^k}=\sum_{k=0}^n\binom nkf^{(n-k)}(x)g^{(k)}(x),$$`
where `$\binom nk$` denotes the [binomial coefficient][bookofproofs$993].
