Let `$(X,d)$` be a [metric space][bookofproofs$617] and let `\(C\subset X\)` be a [compact][bookofproofs$6575] [subset][bookofproofs$552] of `\(X\)`. Then, any [closed][bookofproofs$852] subset `\(A\subset C\)` is also compact.
