A [real infinite series][bookofproofs$1109]  `\(\sum_{k=0}^\infty x_k\)` is [divergent][bookofproofs$217], if the is a divergent series `$\sum_{k=0}^\infty y_k$` such that `$x_k\ge y_k \ge 0$` for all `$k\in\mathbb N.$`
