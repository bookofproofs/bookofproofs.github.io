Let `$f:\mathbb R\to\mathbb R,$` `$f(x)=c$` be a [constant function][bookofproofs$1371]. Then for every `$c\in\mathbb R$` and for every `$a\in\mathbb R,$` `$\lim_{x\to a }f(x)=c.$`
