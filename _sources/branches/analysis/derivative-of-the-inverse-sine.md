The [derivative][bookofproofs$1370] of the [inverse sine][bookofproofs$6748] `$f(x)=\arcsin(x),$` `$-1 < x < 1,$` is given by `$$\arcsin'(x)=\frac1{\cos(\arcsin(x))}.$$`
