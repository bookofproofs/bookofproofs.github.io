The [cosine of a real variable][bookofproofs$1745] is an [even function][bookofproofs$416], i.e. 

`\[\cos(-x)=\cos(x),\quad\quad x\in\mathbb R.\]`

In particular, its graph is symmetric with respect to the `\(y\)`-axis.
