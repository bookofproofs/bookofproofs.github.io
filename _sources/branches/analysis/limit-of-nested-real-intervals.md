Let `$(I_n)_{n\in\mathbb N}$` be [nested real intervals][bookofproofs$8671]. Then there is exactly one [real number][bookofproofs$1105] `$x\in\mathbb R$` being their [limit][bookofproofs$6683] `$$\lim_{n\to\infty}I_{n}=x.$$`
