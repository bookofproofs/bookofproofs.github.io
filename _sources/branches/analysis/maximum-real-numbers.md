Let `\(D\)` be a [non-empty subset][bookofproofs$552] of [real numbers][bookofproofs$1105]. The real number `\(M\)`, denoted by `\(\max(D)\)`, is called the *maximum of `$D$`* if and only if

* `$M$` is a [supremum][bookofproofs$1754] of `$D$`, and
* `$M\in D$`.
