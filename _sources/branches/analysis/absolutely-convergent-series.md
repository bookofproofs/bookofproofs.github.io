A [real series][bookofproofs$1109] `\(\sum_{k=0}^\infty x_k\)` is **absolutely convergent**, if the series of [absolute values][bookofproofs$583] of the summands `\[\sum_{k=0}^\infty |x_k|\]` is [convergent][bookofproofs$175].
