The [cosine function][bookofproofs$1745] has in the [closed interval][bookofproofs$1153] `$[0,2]$` exactly one [zero][bookofproofs$6736] at `$\pi/2.$` Formally, there exists exactly one `$\alpha\in[0,2]$` with `$\cos(\alpha)=0,$` and we have `$\alpha=\pi/2.$`
