Let `\(D\subset\mathbb R\)` be a [subset][bookofproofs$552] of [real numbers][bookofproofs$1105] `$\mathbb R$` and let `$f:D\mapsto \mathbb R$` be a [uniformly continuous function][bookofproofs$6611] on `$D$`. Then `$f$` is [continuous on `$D$`][bookofproofs$1608].
