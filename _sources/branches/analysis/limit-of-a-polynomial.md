Let `$a_n,a_{n-1},\ldots,a_1,a_0\in\mathbb R$` be [real numbers][bookofproofs$1105], `$n$` be a [natural number][bookofproofs$718], and let `$$p:=\begin{cases}\mathbb R&\to\mathbb R,\\x&\to p(x):=a_nx^n+a_{n-1}x^{n-1}+\ldots+a_1x+a_0\end{cases}$$` be a [polynomial][bookofproofs$199] of the degree `$n.$` Then for every `$a\in\mathbb R,$` `$\lim_{x\to a }p(x)=p(a).$`
