The [limits][bookofproofs$6683] of [natural logarithm][bookofproofs$1421] for `$x\to\infty$` and `$x\searrow 0$` are given by
`$$\lim_{x\to\infty}\ln (x)=\infty,$$`
`$$\lim_{x\searrow 0}\ln (x)=-\infty.$$`
