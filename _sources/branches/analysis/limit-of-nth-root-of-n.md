The [limit][bookofproofs$141] of the [nth-root][bookofproofs$46] of a [natural number][bookofproofs$718] `$\sqrt[n]{n}$` for `$n\to\infty$` is equal to `$1,$` formally

`$$\lim_{n\to\infty} \sqrt[n]{n}=1.$$`
