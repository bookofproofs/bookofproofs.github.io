The [limits][bookofproofs$6683] of [general powers][bookofproofs$1626] `$x^\alpha$` and  `$x^{-\alpha}$` for a fixed `$\alpha > 0$` and for `$x\searrow 0$` are given by
`$$\lim_{x\searrow 0}x^\alpha=0,$$`
`$$\lim_{x\searrow 0}\frac 1{x^\alpha}=\infty,$$`
`$$\lim_{x\searrow 0}x^x=1.$$`
