Let `\(I\subseteq\mathbb R\)` be a [subset][bookofproofs$550] of [real numbers][bookofproofs$1105] and let `\(f:I\mapsto\mathbb R\)` be a  [function][bookofproofs$592] `\(f\)` is called:

* **even**, if `\(f(x)=f(-x)\)`, 
* **odd**, if `\(f(x)=-f(-x)\)` 

for all `\(x\in I\)`.
