A [real-valued][bookofproofs$1105] [function][bookofproofs$592] `\(f:\mathbb R\to \mathbb R\)` is called **periodic**, if there is a real number `$a\in\mathbb R,$` such that `$f(x+a)=f(x)$` for all `$x\in\mathbb R.$` The number `$a$` is called the **period** of the function.
