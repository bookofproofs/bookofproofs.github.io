A [complex series][bookofproofs$1724] `\(\sum_{k=0}^\infty x_k\)` is a **absolutely convergent complex series**, if the series of [absolute values][bookofproofs$1247] of the summands `\[\sum_{k=0}^\infty |x_k|\]` is a [convergent complex series][bookofproofs$147].
