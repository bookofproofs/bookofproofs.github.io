The following formula holds for the calculation of the [sum][bookofproofs$261] of arguments of the [hyperbolic cosine][bookofproofs$6687] `$\cosh$`: `$$\cosh(x+y)=\cosh(x)\cosh(y)+\sinh(x)\sinh(y)$$`
for all `$x\in\mathbb R,$` where `$\sinh$` denotes the [hyperbolic sine][bookofproofs$6688].
