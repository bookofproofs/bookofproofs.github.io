Let `\([a,b]\)` be a [closed real interval][bookofproofs$1153] and let `\(f:[a,b]\to\mathbb R\)` be an arbitrary [continuous real function][bookofproofs$1260]. Then `$f$` is [bounded][bookofproofs$302].
