From the [order relation of two real numbers][bookofproofs$1107] `$x < y$` it follows for the [arithmetic mean][bookofproofs$8369] `$\frac{x+y}{2}$` that in lies in between of `$x$` and `$y$`, formally `$$x < \frac{x+y}{2} < y.$$`
