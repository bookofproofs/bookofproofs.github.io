For all [real numbers][bookofproofs$1105] `$x,y\in\mathbb R,$` for which the [tangent functions][bookofproofs$6746] `$\tan(x),$` `$\tan(y),$` and `$\tan(x+y),$` are defined, the following additivity theorem holds:

`$$\tan(x+y)=\frac{\tan(x)+\tan(y)}{1-\tan(x)\tan(y)}.$$`
