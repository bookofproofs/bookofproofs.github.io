For all `\(x,y\in\mathbb R_+^*\)`, i.e. for all [positive real numbers][bookofproofs$1107], the [natural logarithm][bookofproofs$1421] fulfills the following [functional equation][bookofproofs$6726]:

`\[\ln(xy)=\ln(x) + \ln(y).\]`
