The [derivative][bookofproofs$1370] of the [general power function of positive numbers][bookofproofs$1626] `$f(x)=x^a,$` `$x > 0,$` is given for all `$a\in\mathbb R$` by `$$\frac{dx^a}{dx}=ax^{a-1}.$$`
