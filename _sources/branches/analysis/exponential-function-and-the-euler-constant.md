The [exponential function][bookofproofs$281] of an [integer][bookofproofs$844] `$k$` equals the [$k$-th power][bookofproofs$1618] of the [Euler's constant][bookofproofs$1344] `$e$`, i.e. `$\exp(k)=e^k$` for all  `$k\in\mathbb Z.$`
