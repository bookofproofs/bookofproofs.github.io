For all [natural numbers][bookofproofs$718] `$n\ge 4$`, the following inequality holds `$$2^n\le n!.$$`

### Example

For `$n < 4$`, we have 
`$$\begin{array}{rrcc}n&2^n&n !&\text{comparison}\\
0&1&1& = \\
1&2&1& > \\
2&4&2& > \\
3&8&6& > \\
\end{array}$$`
