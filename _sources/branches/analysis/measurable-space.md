A **measurable space** `\((M,\mathcal{A},\mu)\)` is a triple consisting of:

* an underlying [set][bookofproofs$550] `\(M\)`,
* a [`\(\sigma\)`-algebra][bookofproofs$6212] `\(\mathcal{A}\)` defined on it and 
* a [measure][bookofproofs$6232] `\(\mu\)`.
