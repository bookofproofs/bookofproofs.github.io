If `\((c_n)_{n\in\mathbb N}\)` is a [convergent complex sequence][bookofproofs$1700] with `\[\lim_{n\to\infty}c_n=c,\]` then the sequence of [complex conjugates][bookofproofs$1245] `\((\overline{c_n})_{n\in\mathbb N}\)` is also convergent with `\[\lim_{n\to\infty}\overline{c_n}=\overline{c}.\]`
