Let `$a < b$`, let `$[a,b]$` be a [closed real interval][bookofproofs$1153] and let `$f,g:[a,b]\to\mathbb R$` be [Riemann-integrable functions][bookofproofs$1763]. Then the product `$fg$` is Riemann-integrable.
