Let `\(D\subseteq\mathbb C\)` be a [subset][bookofproofs$550] of [complex numbers][bookofproofs$216] and let `\(f:D\mapsto\mathbb C\)` be a function. `\(f\)` is called:

* **even**, if `\(f(z)=f(-z)\)`, 
* **odd**, if `\(f(z)=-f(-z)\)` 

for all `\(z\in D\)`.
