Let `$a,b\in\mathbb R$`. The [Riemann integral][bookofproofs$1763] of [sine][bookofproofs$1746] in the [closed interval][bookofproofs$1153] `$[a,b]$` equals

`$$\int_a^b \sin(x)dx = -\cos(x)\;\begin{array}{|l}a\\\\b\end{array}.$$`
