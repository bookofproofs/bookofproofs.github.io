Let `$f:\mathbb R\to\mathbb R,$` `$f(x)=x$` be a [real identity function][bookofproofs$6680]. Then for every `$a\in\mathbb R,$` `$\lim_{x\to a }f(x)=\lim_{x\to a }x=a.$`
