Given a [constant function][bookofproofs$1371] `\(f:\mathbb R\to\mathbb R\)`, `\(f(x)=c\)`, `\(c\in\mathbb R\)`, and by definition is of the [derivative][bookofproofs$1370] we have

`\[f'(x)=\lim_{\substack{\xi\to x\\\xi\neq x}}\frac {f(\xi)-f(x)}{\xi-x}=\frac{c-c}{\xi-x}=0.\]`
