Let `$\sum_{k=0}^\infty a_k$` and `$\sum_{k=0}^\infty b_k$` be [convergent real series][bookofproofs$175]. Then the [real series][bookofproofs$1109] `$\sum_{k=0}^\infty (a_k+b_k)$` is also [convergent][bookofproofs$175].
