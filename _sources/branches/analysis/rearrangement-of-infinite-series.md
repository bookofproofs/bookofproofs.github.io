Let `\(\sum_{k=0}^\infty x_k\)` be a [real series][bookofproofs$1109] and let `\(\sigma:\mathbb N\to\mathbb N\)` be a [permutation][bookofproofs$188] of its indices. Then the series

`\[\sum_{k=0}^\infty x_{\sigma(k)}\]`

is called the **rearrangement** of the original real series.
