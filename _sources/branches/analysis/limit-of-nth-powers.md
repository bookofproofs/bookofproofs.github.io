Let `$f:\mathbb R\to\mathbb R,$` `$f(x)=x^n$` be a [$n$-th power function][bookofproofs$6680] for any [natural number][bookofproofs$718] `$n\ge 1.$` Then for every `$a\in\mathbb R,$` `$\lim_{x\to a }f(x)=\lim_{x\to a }x^n=a^n.$`
