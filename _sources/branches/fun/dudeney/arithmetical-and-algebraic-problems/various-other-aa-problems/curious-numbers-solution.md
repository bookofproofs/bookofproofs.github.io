The three smallest numbers, in addition to `$48,$` are `$1,680,$` `$57,120,$` and `$1,940,448.$` It will be found that the numbers are the respective squares:

* `$1,681=41^2,$`
* `$841=29^2,$`
* `$57,121=239^2,$`
* `$28,561=169^2$`,
* `$1,940,449=1,393^2$` and
* `$970,225=985^2.$`
