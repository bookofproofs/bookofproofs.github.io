If you take a rectangular piece of cardboard, twice as long as it is broad, and cut it in half diagonally, you will get two of the pieces shown in the illustration. 

![q147](https://github.com/bookofproofs/bookofproofs.github.io/blob/main/_sources/_assets/images/dudeney/q147.png?raw=true)

The puzzle is with five such pieces of equal size to form a [square][bookofproofs$909] One of the pieces may be cut in two, but the others must be used intact.
