Since `\(S=\underbrace{A\times \ldots\times A}_{n \text{ times}}\)`, it follows from the [fundamental counting principle][bookofproofs$111] that 

`\[|S|=\prod_{1}^n|A|=\prod_{1}^n k=k^n.\]`
