The following identities hold for [binomial coefficients][bookofproofs$1400]:

1. `$$k\binom nk=n\binom {n-1}{k-1}\quad\quad\text{for }k\ge 1.$$`
1. `$$k^2\binom nk=k\binom nk+n(n-1)\binom {n-2}{k-2}\quad\quad\text{for }k\ge 2.$$`
1. `$$k^2\binom nk=k\binom nk+n(n-1)\binom {n-2}{k-2}\quad\quad\text{for }k\ge 2.$$`
