Let `$n\ge 1$` be a [positive integer][bookofproofs$1075] and let `$x\in \mathbb C$` be a [complex number][bookofproofs$6788]. Then the [difference operator][bookofproofs$8408] of the [nth power][bookofproofs$1618] `$x^n$` equals

`$$\Delta x^n=(x+1)^n-x^n=\sum_{k=1}^n \binom {n}{k} x^{n-k}.$$`
