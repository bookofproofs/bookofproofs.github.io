An [ideal][bookofproofs$1062] `$I\lhd R$` in a [commutative ring][bookofproofs$880] `$(R, + ,\cdot)$` is called a **prime ideal**, if 

* `$I\neq R$` and 
* for any `$r,s\in R$` with `$rs\in I$` it follows `$r\in I$` or `$s\in I.$`
