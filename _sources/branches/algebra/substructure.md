A **substructure** `$(S,\ast)$` is a [proper subset][bookofproofs$552] of a given [algebraic structure][bookofproofs$342] `$(X,\ast)$` which is [closed][bookofproofs$1103] under the [binary operation][bookofproofs$6188] `$"\ast".$`
