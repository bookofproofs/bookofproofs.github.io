In every [group][bookofproofs$671] `$(G,\ast),$` the equation `\(a\ast x=b\)` is uniquely solvable for all `$a,b\in G$` and its solution is `$x=a^{-1}\ast b$` where `$a^{-1}$` is the [inverse element][bookofproofs$670] of `$a$` in `$G.$`
