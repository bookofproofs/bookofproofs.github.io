Let `$(R, + ,\cdot)$` be an [integral domain][bookofproofs$821], `$a\in R$` with `$a\neq 0.$` A [principal ideal][bookofproofs$1063] `$(a)\lhd R$` is a [prime ideal][bookofproofs$6240] if and only if `$a$` is [prime][bookofproofs$822] in `$R.$`
