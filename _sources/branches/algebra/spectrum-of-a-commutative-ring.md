The [set][bookofproofs$550] of all [prime ideals][bookofproofs$6240] of a [commutative ring][bookofproofs$880] `\(R\)`  is called the *spectrum of `\(R\)`*, denoted by `\(\operatorname{Spec}(R)\)`.
