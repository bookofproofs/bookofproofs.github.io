Let `\((G,\ast)\)` be a [group][bookofproofs$671] and `\(H\)` be a non-empty subset `\(H\subseteq G\)`. 

1. `\(H\)` is of a  [subgroup][bookofproofs$554] of `\(G\)`, if and only if `\(a\ast b^{-1}\in H\)` for all `\(a,b\in H\)`.
1. `\(H\)` is of a  [subgroup][bookofproofs$554] of `\(G\)`, if and only if `\(a\ast b^{-1}\in H\)` for all `\(a,b\in H\)`.
