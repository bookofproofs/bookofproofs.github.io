An [ideal][bookofproofs$1062] `\(I\lhd R\)` in a  [commutative ring][bookofproofs$880] `\(R\)` is called a **maximal ideal**, if 
* `\(I\neq R\)` and
* there is no [proper superset][bookofproofs$552] of `\(J\supset I\)` and an ideal of `$J\lhd R.$`
