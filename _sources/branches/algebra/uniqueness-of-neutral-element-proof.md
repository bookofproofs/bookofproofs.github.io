* Let `$(X,\ast)$` be an [algebraic structure][bookofproofs$342] and let `$e,f$` be [neutral][bookofproofs$661] in `$X.$`
* By definition of neutral, we have `$f=f\ast e=e\ast f=e.$`
* Therefore, `$e$` is unique.
