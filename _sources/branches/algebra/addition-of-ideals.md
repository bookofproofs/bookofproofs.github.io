Let `\(I\lhd R\)` and `\(J\lhd R\)` be two [ideals][bookofproofs$1062] of the ring `\(R\)`. Then the set 

`\[I+J:=\{i+j~|~i\in I, j\in J\}\]`
is called the **sum** of the two ideals.
