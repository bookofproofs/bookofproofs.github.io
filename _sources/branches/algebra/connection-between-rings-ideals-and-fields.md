1. Every [field][bookofproofs$557] `$(F, + ,\cdot)$` has only the  two [ideals][bookofproofs$1062]: `$\{0\}\lhd F$` and `$F\lhd F$` (i.e. the ideal `$\{0\}$` consisting of only the [zero element][bookofproofs$661] `$0\in F$`) and the field `$F$` itself).
1. Every [field][bookofproofs$557] `$(F, + ,\cdot)$` has only the  two [ideals][bookofproofs$1062]: `$\{0\}\lhd F$` and `$F\lhd F$` (i.e. the ideal `$\{0\}$` consisting of only the [zero element][bookofproofs$661] `$0\in F$`) and the field `$F$` itself).
