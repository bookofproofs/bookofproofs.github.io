Let `\((F, + ,\cdot)\)` be a [field][bookofproofs$683] A [subset][bookofproofs$552]  `\(S\subseteq R\)` is called a **subfield** of `\(F\)`, if `\((S, + ,\cdot)\)` itself is a field.
