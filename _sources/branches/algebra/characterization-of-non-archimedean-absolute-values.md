1. If `$|\cdot|$` is a [non-archimedean absolute value][bookofproofs$8659] of a field `$(F,+,\cdot)$`, then `$|n|\le 1$` for all multiples of `$1\in F$`, i.e. `$$n=\underbrace{1+\ldots+1}_{n\text{ times}},\quad 1\in F.$$`
