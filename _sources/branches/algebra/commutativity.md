Let `$(X,\ast)$` be an [algebraic structure][bookofproofs$85] The [binary operation][bookofproofs$6188] `$"\ast"$` is called **commutative** if `$x\ast y=y\ast y$` holds for any two elements `\(x,y\in X\)`.
