Let `\(S\)` be an [algebra over the ring ][bookofproofs$6213] `\(R\)`. The [set][bookofproofs$550] of all [integral elements][bookofproofs$6258] `\(x\in S\)` is called the **integral closure** of `\(R\)` in `\(S\)`.
