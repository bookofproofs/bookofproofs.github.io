A **square matrix** `\(A\in M_{m\times n}(F)\)` is a [matrix][bookofproofs$1048], in which the number `\(n\)` of columns and the number `\(m\)` of rows are the same (`\(m=n\)`):
`\[A\in M_{n\times n}(F).\]`
