Every binary [relations][bookofproofs$571] `$R\subseteq S\times T$` has an **inverse relation**  `$R^{-1}\subseteq T\times S$` which is defined by the [equivalence][bookofproofs$574] `$$tR^{-1}s\Leftrightarrow sRt$$`
for all `$s\in S$` and `$t\in T.$`
