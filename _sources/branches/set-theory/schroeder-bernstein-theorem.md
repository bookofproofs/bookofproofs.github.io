Let `$A$` and `$B$` be [sets][bookofproofs$550] with the [cardinalities][bookofproofs$980]  `$|A|$` and `$|B|$`. If `$|A|\le|B|$` and `$|B|\le|A|,$` then both sets are [equipotent][bookofproofs$978], i.e. `$|A|=|B|.$`
