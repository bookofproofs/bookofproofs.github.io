Let `\(A\)` und `\(B\)` be [sets][bookofproofs$550] and let `\(c\in B\)` be an element. A [function][bookofproofs$592] `\(f:A\mapsto B\)` with `\(f(x)=c\)` for all `$x\in A$` is called **constant**.
