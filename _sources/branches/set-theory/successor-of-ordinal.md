Let `\(\alpha\in\Omega\)` be an [ordinal number][bookofproofs$723]. Then the set 
`\[s(\alpha):=\alpha\cup\{\alpha\}\]`
is again an ordinal number and is called the **successor** of `\(\alpha\)`.
