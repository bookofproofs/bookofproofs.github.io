For all sets `$A,B,C$` the following [distributivity][bookofproofs$682] laws hold for the [set union][bookofproofs$6827] "`$\cup$`" and the [set intersection][bookofproofs$6828] "`$\cap$`":

1. `$A\cap (B\cup C)=(A\cap B) \cup (A\cap C),$`
1. `$A\cap (B\cup C)=(A\cap B) \cup (A\cap C),$`
