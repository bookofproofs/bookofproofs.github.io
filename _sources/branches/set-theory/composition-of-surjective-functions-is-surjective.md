Let `$A,B,C$` be [sets][bookofproofs$550] and let `$f:A\to B$` and `$g:B\to C$` be [surjective functions][bookofproofs$770]. Then the [composition][bookofproofs$1314] `$(g\circ f):A\to C$` is also surjective.
