Let `\(S_1, \ldots, S_n\)` be (not necessarily different), `$n\ge 1$`, [sets][bookofproofs$550]. Any [subset][bookofproofs$552] of the [Cartesian product][bookofproofs$748] `\(R\subseteq S_1\times \ldots\times S_n\)` is called an *$n$-ary relation* or just a **relation**.
