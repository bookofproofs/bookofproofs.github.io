Let `$A,B$` be [sets][bookofproofs$550].
1. If `$A$` has an empty [intersection][bookofproofs$6828] with `$B,$` then `$A$` is contained in the complement of `$B,$` formally `$$A\cap B=\emptyset\Rightarrow A\subseteq B^C.$$`
1. If `$A$` has an empty [intersection][bookofproofs$6828] with `$B,$` then `$A$` is contained in the complement of `$B,$` formally `$$A\cap B=\emptyset\Rightarrow A\subseteq B^C.$$`
