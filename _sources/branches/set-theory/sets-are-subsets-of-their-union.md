Let `$A$` and `$B$` be [sets][bookofproofs$550]. Then both, `$A$` and `$B$`, are the [subsets][bookofproofs$552] of their [union][bookofproofs$6827] `$A\cup B$`, formally `$A\subseteq A\cup B$` and  `$B\subseteq A\cup B$`.
