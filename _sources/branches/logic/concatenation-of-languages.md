Let `$L_1, L_2$` be [languages][bookofproofs$7842] over some [alphabets][bookofproofs$708]. The **concatenation** "`$\cdot$`" of the languages `$L_1, L_2$` is a language defined as `$$L_1L_2=L_1\cdot L_2:=\{uw\mid u\in L_1, w\in L_2\}.$$`
