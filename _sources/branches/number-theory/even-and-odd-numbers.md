An [integer][bookofproofs$844] `$n$` is called **even**, if it is [divisible][bookofproofs$700] by `$2$` (i.e. `$2\mid n$`), otherwise (i.e. if `$2\not\mid n$`) it is called *odd.*

### Examples

* `$0,2,10,-20,250,\ldots$` are even.
* `$1,-1,3,-3,255,-33,\ldots$` are odd.
