Let `$a,b$` be [integers][bookofproofs$844] and `$n > 0, m > 0$` be [positive integers][bookofproofs$1075] with `$m\mid n,$` i.e. `$m$` being a [divisor][bookofproofs$700] of `$n.$` Then 
`$$\begin{array}{c}a(n)\equiv b(n)\\
\Longrightarrow\\
(a)(m)\equiv(b)(m).
\end{array}$$`
