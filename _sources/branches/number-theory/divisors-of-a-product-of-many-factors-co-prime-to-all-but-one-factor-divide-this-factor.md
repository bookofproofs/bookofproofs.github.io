This is a [generalization of the corresponding theorem for two factors][bookofproofs$1293]. If `\[a|\prod_{\nu=1}^n a_\nu,\quad n\ge 2\]`
and if `\[a\perp a_\nu,\quad 1\le \nu < n,\]`
then 
`\[a|a_n.\]`
