Let `$a,b$` be [integers][bookofproofs$844] and `$c > 0, m > 0$` be [positive integers][bookofproofs$1075]. Then `$$\begin{array}{c}a(m)\equiv b(m)\\
\Longleftrightarrow\\(ac)(mc)\equiv(bc)(mc).
\end{array}$$`
