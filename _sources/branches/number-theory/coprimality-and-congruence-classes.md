Let `$m > 0$` be a [positive integer][bookofproofs$1075]. Among the infinitely many representatives `$a\in\mathbb Z$` of a given [congruence class][bookofproofs$8154] `$a(m)\in\mathbb Z_m$`, either all or none are [co-prime][bookofproofs$8093] to `$m.$`
