Let `\(a,b\in\mathbb Z\)` and let `\(a\)` be a [divisor][bookofproofs$700] of `\(b\)`. From the divisibility `\(a\mid b\)` the following divisibility relations follow: `\(a\mid -b~\)`, `\(~-a\mid b~\)`,`\(~-a\mid -b\)` and `\(|a|~\mid ~|b|\)`.
