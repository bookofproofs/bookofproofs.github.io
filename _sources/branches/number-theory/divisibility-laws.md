The following laws of [divisibility][bookofproofs$700] hold in `\(\mathbb Z\)`:

1. `\((1\mid n\wedge n\mid  n\wedge n\mid 0)\quad\forall n\in\mathbb Z\)`.
1. `\((m\mid  n \wedge n\mid  m) \Rightarrow n=m\)`.
1. `\((m\mid  n \wedge n\mid  l) \Rightarrow m\mid  l\)`.
1. `\(m\mid  n\ \Rightarrow (m\mid (nr)\quad\forall r\in\mathbb Z)\)`.
1. `\((m\mid  n \wedge m\mid  l) \Rightarrow m\mid  (n+l)\)`.
1. `\((m\mid  n \wedge m\mid  (n+l)) \Rightarrow m\mid  l\)`.
1. `\(mr\mid nr  \Rightarrow m\mid n\)`.
1. `\((m\mid n\wedge r\neq 0) \Rightarrow mr\mid nr\)`.
1. `\((m\mid n\wedge r\neq 0) \Rightarrow mr\mid nr\)`.
