According to the International System of Units (SI), one **meter** is 

>  the distance travelled by light in a vacuum in `\(1 / 299\,792\,458\)` [seconds][bookofproofs$6273].
This definition is permissible because of the [principle of the constancy of speed of light][bookofproofs$6272].
