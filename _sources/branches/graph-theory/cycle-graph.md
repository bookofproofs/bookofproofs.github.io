A [simple undirected graph][bookofproofs$523] `\(G(V,E)\)` with `\(|V|=n\)` vertices is called a **cycle graph**, if it consists of a single [cycle][bookofproofs$1165] of vertices and edges.
 
The cycle graph with `\(n\)` vertices is denoted by `\(C_n\)`.
