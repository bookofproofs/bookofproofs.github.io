A *semi-Eulerian tour* in a [simple graph][bookofproofs$523] `\(G(V,E)\)` is a [trail][bookofproofs$1164] that includes every edge but which is not a [closed trail][bookofproofs$1165]. Such a trail is called a **open trail**.
