Let `$G$` be a [simple][bookofproofs$523], [connected][bookofproofs$1166], [planar][bookofproofs$1226] graph. Then the [chromatic number][bookofproofs$8448] of `$G$` obeys the following inequality `$$\chi(G)\le 5.$$`
