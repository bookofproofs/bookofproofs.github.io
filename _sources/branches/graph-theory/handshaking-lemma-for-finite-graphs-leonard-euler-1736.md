In any finite [graph][bookofproofs$523] `\(G(V,E,\gamma)\)`, the sum of all the [vertex degrees][bookofproofs$362] is equal to twice the number of edges:
`\[\sum_{v\in V}\deg_G(v)=2|E|.\]`
