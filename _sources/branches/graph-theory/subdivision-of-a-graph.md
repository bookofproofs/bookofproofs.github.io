Any [undirected graph][bookofproofs$523] `\(H\)` formed from another undirected graph `\(G\)` by inserting vertices of [degree][bookofproofs$362] `\(2\)` into the edges of `\(G\)` is called a **subdivision** of `\(G\)`.
