If `\(G\)` is a [planar graph][bookofproofs$1226], then every [subdivision][bookofproofs$6377] of `\(G\)` is planar.

Equivalently,

If `\(G\)` is a subdivision of a non-planar graph, then `\(G\)` is non-planar.
