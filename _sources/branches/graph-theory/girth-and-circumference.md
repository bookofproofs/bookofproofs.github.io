Let `\(G(V,E)\)` be a [cyclic graph][bookofproofs$6376] `\(G\)`.

The **girth** of `\(G\)` is the length of the __shortest__ cycle in `\(G\)`.

The **circumference** of `\(G\)` is the length of the __longest__ cycle in `\(G\)`.
