Let `\(n,k\in\mathbb N\)` be [natural numbers][bookofproofs$718] and let `\(a_k \in F\)` be any elements of a given [field][bookofproofs$557] `\((F, +, \cdot)\)`. 

For a sum `\[S_n:=\sum_{0\le k\le n} a_k\]` the following property holds:

`\[S_n+a_{n+1}=a_0+\sum_{0\le k\le n} a_{k+1}.\]`
