There exists a [natural number][bookofproofs$718] `\(0\in\mathbb N\)` such that `\[x+0=0+x=x\]` for all `\(x\in\mathbb N\)`, i.e. `\(0\)` is neutral with respect to the [addition of natural numbers][bookofproofs$842].
