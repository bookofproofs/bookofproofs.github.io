There exists a [complex number][bookofproofs$216] `\(0\in\mathbb C\)` such that `\[x+0=0+x=0\]` for all `\(x\in\mathbb C\)`, i.e. `\(0\)` is neutral with respect to the [addition or complex numbers][bookofproofs$1657].
