For [natural numbers][bookofproofs$718] `\(n\ge k\)`, the following [sum][bookofproofs$216] formula for [binomial coefficients][bookofproofs$993] holds 

`\[\sum_{m=k}^n \binom mk=\binom{n+1}{k+1}.\]`
