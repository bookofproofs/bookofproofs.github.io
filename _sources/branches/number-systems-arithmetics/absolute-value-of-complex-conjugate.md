For all `$z\in\mathbb C$`, the [absolute value][bookofproofs$1247] of the [complex conjugate][bookofproofs$1245] `$z^*$` equals the absolute value of `$z$`, formally `$$|z^*|=|z|=\sqrt{\Re(z)^2+\Im(z)^2}.$$`
