There exists an [integer][bookofproofs$844] `\(1\in\mathbb Z\)` such that `\[x\cdot 1=1\cdot x=x\]` for all `\(x\in\mathbb Z\)`, i.e. `\(1\)` is neutral with respect to the [multiplication or integers][bookofproofs$891].
