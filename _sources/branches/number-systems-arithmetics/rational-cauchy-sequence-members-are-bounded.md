For any given [rational Cauchy sequence][bookofproofs$1485] `\((a_n)_{n\in\mathbb N}\)`, the rational numbers `\(a_n\)` are **bounded**, i.e. there exists a positive constant `\(c\in\mathbb Q\)`, such that `\(|a_n|\le c\)` for all `\(n\in\mathbb N\)`.
