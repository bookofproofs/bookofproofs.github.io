For any element of a [ring][bookofproofs$683] `\(x\in(R,+,\cdot) \)` and any [natural number][bookofproofs$718] `\(n\ge 1\)` the following [sum][bookofproofs$216] formula holds:

`\[\sum_{k=0}^nk\binom nk(1-x)^{n-k}x^k=nx.\]`
