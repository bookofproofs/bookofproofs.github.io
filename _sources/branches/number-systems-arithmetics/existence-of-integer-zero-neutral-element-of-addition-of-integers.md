There exists an [integer][bookofproofs$844] `\(0\in\mathbb Z\)` such that `\[x+0=0+x=x\]` for all `\(x\in\mathbb Z\)`, i.e. `\(0\)` is neutral with respect to the [addition or integers][bookofproofs$890].
