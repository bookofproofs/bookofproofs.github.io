There exists a [real number][bookofproofs$1105] `\(1\in\mathbb R\)` such that `\[x\cdot 1=1\cdot x=x\]` for all `\(x\in\mathbb R\)`, i.e. `\(1\)` is neutral with respect to the [multiplication or real numbers][bookofproofs$1532].
