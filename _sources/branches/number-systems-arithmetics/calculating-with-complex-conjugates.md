For any complex numbers `\(z, z_1,z_2\in\mathbb C\)` we have

> `\((1)\)` `\((z^*)^*=z\)`.

> `\((2)\)` `\((z_1+z_2)^*=z_1^*+z_2^*\)`.

> `\((3)\)` `\((z_1\cdot z_2)^*=z_1^*\cdot z_2^*\)`.
