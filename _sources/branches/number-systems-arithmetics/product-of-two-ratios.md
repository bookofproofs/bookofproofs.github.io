Let `\(a,b,c,d\)` be [real numbers][bookofproofs$1105] with `\(b\neq 0\)` and `\(d\neq 0\)`. Then the [product][bookofproofs$1532] of the [ratios][bookofproofs$6634] `$\frac ab$` and `$\frac cd$` equals the ratio `$\frac {ac}{bd}$`, formally `$$\frac ab\cdot\frac cd=\frac {a\cdot c}{b\cdot d}.$$`
