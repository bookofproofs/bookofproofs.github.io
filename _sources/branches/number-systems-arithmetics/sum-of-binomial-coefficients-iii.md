For any element of a [ring][bookofproofs$683] `\(x\in(R,+,\cdot) \)` and any [natural number][bookofproofs$718] `\(n\ge 0\)` the following [sum][bookofproofs$216] formula holds:

`\[\sum_{k=0}^nk^2\binom nk(1-x)^{n-k}x^k=nx(1-x)+n^2x^2.\]`
