Given natural numbers `\(x,y\)`, the relationship between the relations "`\( > \)`" and  "`\( \ge \)`" as well as the relations "`\( < \)`" and  "`\( \le \)`" is as follows:

* `\(y > x\)` is equivalent to `\(y \ge  x +1\)`.
* `\(y < x\)` is equivalent to `\(y +1 \le x\)`.
