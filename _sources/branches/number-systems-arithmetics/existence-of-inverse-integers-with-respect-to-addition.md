For every [integer][bookofproofs$844] `\(x\in\mathbb Z\)` there exists an **inverse integer** `\(-x\in\mathbb Z\)` such that the [sum of both integers][bookofproofs$890] equals the [integer zero][bookofproofs$1452]:

`\[x+(-x)=0.\]`
