For all elements `$a,b\in R$` of a [unit ring][bookofproofs$683] `$R$` and all [natural numbers][bookofproofs$718] `$n$`, the following [sum][bookofproofs$261] formula (called **geomertic sum**) holds:

`$$(a-b)\sum_{k=0}^n a^k\cdot b^{n-k}=a^{n+1}-b^{n+1}.$$`
