For every [rational number][bookofproofs$1485] `\(x\in\mathbb Q\)` there exists an **inverse rational number** `\(-x\in\mathbb Q\)` such that the [sum of both numbers][bookofproofs$1486] equals the [rational zero][bookofproofs$1498]:

`\[x+(-x)=0.\]`
