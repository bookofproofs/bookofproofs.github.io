There exists a [rational number][bookofproofs$1033] `\(0\in\mathbb Q\)` such that `\[x+0=0+x=x\]` for all `\(x\in\mathbb Q\)`, i.e. `\(0\)` is neutral with respect to the [addition or rational numbers][bookofproofs$1446].
