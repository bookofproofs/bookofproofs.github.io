Let `\(a,b,c,d\)` be [real numbers][bookofproofs$1105] with `\(b\neq 0,c\neq 0,d\neq 0\)`. Then the [ratio][bookofproofs$6634] of the [ratios][bookofproofs$6634] `$\frac ab$` and `$\frac cd$` equals the ratio `$\frac {ad}{bc}$`, formally `$$\frac{\frac ab}{\frac cd}=\frac {a\cdot d}{b\cdot c}.$$`
