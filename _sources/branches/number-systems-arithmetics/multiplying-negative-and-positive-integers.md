The [multiplication][bookofproofs$891] of [negative and positive integers][bookofproofs$1075] follows the following rules:

* A positive integer times a positive integer gives a positive integer, symbolically
`\[( + )\cdot ( + )=( + )\]`
* A negative integer times a positive integer gives a negative integer.
`\[( - )\cdot ( + )=( - )\]`
* A negative integer times a negative integer gives a positive integer.
`\[( - )\cdot ( - )=( + )\]`
* Zero times any integer gives zero.
`\[( 0 )\cdot ( \pm )=( 0 )\]`
