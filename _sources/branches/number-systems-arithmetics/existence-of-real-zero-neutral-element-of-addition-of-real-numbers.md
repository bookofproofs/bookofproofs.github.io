There exists a [real number][bookofproofs$1105] `\(0\in\mathbb R\)` such that `\[x+0=0+x=x\]` for all `\(x\in\mathbb R\)`, i.e. `\(0\)` is neutral with respect to the [addition or real numbers][bookofproofs$1514].
