Let `\(A_1,...,A_n\)` be [mutually independent events][bookofproofs$1808]. By replacing any `\(A_i\)` by the [complement events][bookofproofs$861] `\(\overline{A_i}\)`, `\(1\le i\le n\)`, we obtain mutually independent events again.
