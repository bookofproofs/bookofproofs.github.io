For any two [events][bookofproofs$857] `\(A,B\subseteq\Omega\)` with `\(A\subset B\)` we have
`\[p(A)=p(B)-p(\overline A\cap B).\]`
In particular, it follows that 
`\[p(A)\le p(B).\]`
