If `$F$` is a [filter][bookofproofs$8594] on a [set][bookofproofs$550] `$X$` and there is no filter `$G$` on `$X$` that is [strictly finer][bookofproofs$8630] than `$F$`, then `$F$` is called an **ultrafilter** on `$X.$`
