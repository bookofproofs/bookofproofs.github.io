A [subset][bookofproofs$552] `\(T\subseteq M\)` of a [metric space][bookofproofs$617] `\((M,d)\)` is called **bounded**, if there is a [real number][bookofproofs$6194] `\(b\)` with 

`\[d\left(x,y\right)\leq b\]`

for all `\(x,y\in T\)`.
