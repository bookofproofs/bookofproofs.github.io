Let `$(X,\mathcal O)$` be a [topological space][bookofproofs$6189] `$X$` is a [$T_2$ space][bookofproofs$6199] if and only if every [filter base][bookofproofs$8595] has only one unique [limit point][bookofproofs$8573].
