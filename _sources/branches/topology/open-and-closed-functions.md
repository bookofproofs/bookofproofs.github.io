Let `$(X,\mathcal O_X)$` and `$(Y,\mathcal O_Y)$` be [topological spaces][bookofproofs$6189]. A function `$f:X\to Y$` is said to be **open**  **closed**) if the [image][bookofproofs$592] `$f[A]$` of each open set `$A$` in `$X$` is open (closed) in `$Y.$`
