Let `\((X,d_X)\)` and `\((Y,d_Y)\)` be [metric spaces][bookofproofs$617] and let 

`\[f:X\mapsto Y\]`

be an [isometry][bookofproofs$2778]. Then `\(f\)` is [injective][bookofproofs$769].
