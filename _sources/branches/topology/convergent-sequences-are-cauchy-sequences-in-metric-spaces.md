Let `\((X,d)\)` be a [mectric space][bookofproofs$617]  and let `\((a_n)_{n\in\mathbb N}\)` be a [convergent sequence][bookofproofs$148] of points in `\(X\)`. Then the sequence `\((a_n)\)` is also a [Cauchy sequence][bookofproofs$1072].
