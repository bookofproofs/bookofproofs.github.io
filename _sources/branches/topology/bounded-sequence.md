A [sequence][bookofproofs$874] `$(x_n)_{n\in\mathbb N}$` of points of a metric space `$(X,d)$` is called **bounded**, if the [subset][bookofproofs$552].
`\[\{x_n:~n\in\mathbb N\}\subset X\]`
is [bounded][bookofproofs$6574].
