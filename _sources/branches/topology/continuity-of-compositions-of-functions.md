Let `$(X,\mathcal O_X),$` `$(Y,\mathcal O_Y),$` and `$(Z,\mathcal O_Z)$` be [topological spaces][bookofproofs$6189]. Let `$f:X\to Y$` and `$g:Y\to Z$` be [continuous functions][bookofproofs$8583]. Then the [composition][bookofproofs$1314] `$g\circ f:X\to Z$` is continuous.
