Let `$(X,\mathcal O)$` be a [topological space][bookofproofs$6189] `$X$` is a [$T_1$ space][bookofproofs$6199] if and only if every [singleton][bookofproofs$8034] [subset][bookofproofs$552] of  `$X$` is [closed][bookofproofs$853].
