Let `$(X,\mathcal O_X)$` and `$(Y,\mathcal O_Y)$` be [topological spaces][bookofproofs$6189]. A [bijective function][bookofproofs$771] `$f:X\to Y$` is an [open function][bookofproofs$8586] if and only if its [inverse function][bookofproofs$407] is [continuous][bookofproofs$8583].
