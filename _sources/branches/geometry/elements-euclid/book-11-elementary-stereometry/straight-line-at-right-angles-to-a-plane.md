### (Definition 3 from Book 11 of Euclid's “Elements”)

> A **straight line is at right angles to a plane** when it makes [right angles][bookofproofs$653] with all of the [straight lines][bookofproofs$645] joined to it which are also in the [plane][bookofproofs$647].
