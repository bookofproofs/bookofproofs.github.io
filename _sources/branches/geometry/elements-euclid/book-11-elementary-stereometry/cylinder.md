### (Definition 21 from Book 11 of Euclid's “Elements”)

> A **cylinder** is the [(solid) figure][bookofproofs$2210] enclosed when, one of the sides of a [rectangle][bookofproofs$909] about the [right angle][bookofproofs$653] remaining (fixed), the parallelogram is carried around, and again established at the same (position) from which it began to be moved.
