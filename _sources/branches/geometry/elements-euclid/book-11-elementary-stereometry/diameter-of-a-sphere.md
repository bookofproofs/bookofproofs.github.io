### (Definition 17 from Book 11 of Euclid's “Elements”)

> And the **diameter of the sphere** is any [straight line][bookofproofs$645] which is drawn through the [center][bookofproofs$2225] (of the sphere) and terminated in both directions by the [surface][bookofproofs$2211] of the [sphere][bookofproofs$2223].
