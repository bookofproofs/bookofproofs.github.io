> A **sphere** is the [(solid) figure][bookofproofs$2210] enclosed when, the [diameter][bookofproofs$6429] of a [semicircle][bookofproofs$6430] remaining (fixed), the semicircle is carried around, and again established at the same (position) from which it began to be moved.
