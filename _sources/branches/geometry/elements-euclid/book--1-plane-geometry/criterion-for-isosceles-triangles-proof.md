"`\(\Rightarrow\)`": Let a triangle be isosceles. Then, by virtue of [Prop. 1.5][bookofproofs$740], the angles at its base are equal.

"`\(\Leftarrow\)`": Let the angles at the base of a triangle be equal. Then, by virtue of [Prop 1.6][bookofproofs$743] it is isosceles.
