A **decagon** is a [convex][bookofproofs$6287] [rectilinear figure][bookofproofs$687] with 10 sides and 10 vertices.

A decagon is called **equilateral**, if all of its sides are [congruent][bookofproofs$2781].
A decagon is called **equiangular**, if all of its angles are congruent.
