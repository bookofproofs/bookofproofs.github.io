If the segments `\(\overline{BA}\)` and `\(\overline{CF}\)` used to prove the [proposition 1.16][bookofproofs$784] were extended to straight lines `\(BA\)` and `\(CF\)`, those straight lines could not meet at any finite distance. In other words, they are [parallel][bookofproofs$788].
