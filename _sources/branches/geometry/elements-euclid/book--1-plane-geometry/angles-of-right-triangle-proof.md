* By [Prop 1.32][bookofproofs$924], the sum of all angles in a plane triangle is `$\alpha+\beta+\gamma=180^\circ.$`
* Without loss of generality, let `$\alpha+\gamma=\beta.$`
* Then `$2\beta=180^\circ,$` and `$\beta=90$` is a [right angle][bookofproofs$653].
