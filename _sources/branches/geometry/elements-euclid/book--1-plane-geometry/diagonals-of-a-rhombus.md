The diagonals of a rhombus bisect its angles and intersect at right angles:

* `\(\angle{BAC}\)` is double the size of `\(\angle{BAE}\)`.
* `\(\angle{DBA}\)` is double the size of `\(\angle{DBE}\)`.
* `\(\beta=\angle{BED}=90^\circ\)`.
