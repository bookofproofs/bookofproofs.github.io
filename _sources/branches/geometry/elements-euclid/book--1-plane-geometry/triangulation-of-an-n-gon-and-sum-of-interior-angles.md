If a convex [n-sided figure][bookofproofs$687] is triangulated, the sum of its angles equals `\(2(n-2)\)` [right angles][bookofproofs$653].
`\[\sum \alpha_i=(n-2)\times 180^\circ=2(n-2)\times 90^\circ\]`
