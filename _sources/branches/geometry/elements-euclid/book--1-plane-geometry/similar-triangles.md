If two triangles have two angles in one respectively equal to two angles in the other `\((\alpha_1=\alpha_2\)`, `\(\beta_1=\beta_2)\)`, then they are [similar][bookofproofs$2782], i.e. their remaining pair of angles is also equal `\((\gamma_1=\gamma_2)\)`.
