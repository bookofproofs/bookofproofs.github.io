### (Proposition 10 from Book 6 of Euclid's “Elements”)

> To cut a given uncut [straight line][bookofproofs$645] similarly to a given cut ([straight line][bookofproofs$645]).


![fig10e](https://github.com/bookofproofs/bookofproofs.github.io/blob/main/_sources/_assets/images/euclid/Book06/fig10e.png?raw=true)


### Modern Formulation

It is possible to scale a yardstick (with an arbitrary scale) of a given length `$|AC|$` to the new length `$|AB|.$`
