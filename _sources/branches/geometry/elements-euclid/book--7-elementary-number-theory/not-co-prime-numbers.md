> And [numbers][bookofproofs$2315] **composite to one another** are those (which are) measured by some number as a common measure.

### Modern Formulation

Negation of [co-prime][bookofproofs$8093], formally,
`\[a\not\perp b\Longleftrightarrow\gcd(a,b) > 1.\]`
