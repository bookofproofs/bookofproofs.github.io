### (Proposition 23 from Book 3 of Euclid's “Elements”)

> Two similar and unequal [segments][bookofproofs$1852] of [circles][bookofproofs$690] cannot be constructed on the same side of the same [straight line][bookofproofs$645].
### Modern Formulation

The [arc][bookofproofs$1852] on a given base is unique.
