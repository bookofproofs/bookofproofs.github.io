### (Proposition 13 from Book 3 of Euclid's “Elements”)

> A [circle][bookofproofs$690] [does not touch a(nother) ][bookofproofs$1851] [circle][bookofproofs$690] at more than one [point][bookofproofs$631], whether they touch internally or externally.

### Modern Formulation

Two circles [touch][bookofproofs$1851] one another (internally or externally) at exactly one [point][bookofproofs$631].
