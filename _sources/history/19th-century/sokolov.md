Yurii Dmitrievich Sokolov was a Russian mathematician who worked on the n-body problem.

Born 26 May 1896 Labinskaya Stanitsa (now Labinsk), Russia. Died 2 February 1971 Kiev, Ukranian SSR (now Kyiv, Ukraine).
