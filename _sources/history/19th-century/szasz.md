**Otto Szász** was a Hungarian mathematician who worked on real analysis, especially Fourier series.

Born 11 December 1884 Alsószúcs, Hungary (now Dolná Súča, Slovakia). Died 19 December 1952 Cincinnati, Ohio, USA.
