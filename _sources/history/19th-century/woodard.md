**Dudley Woodard** was an American mathematician and educator who was the second African-American to earn a PhD in Mathematics.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed the Woodard Blackboard System, a set of mathematical algorithms designed to analyze complex real-world problems.
2. Helped develop the Cybernetic Machine Language, the computer programming language used by the Cybernetic Computer.
3. Co-founded Algorithm Solutions Corporation (ASC), a consulting firm devoted to solving complex mathematical problems.
4. Created new mathematical algorithms for solving financial, marketing, and other business problems.
5. Authored several mathematical textbooks, including "Mathematics for the Business Professional" and "Calculus for the Business Professional."
6. Developed the concept of 'market network modelling' to help businesses better understand the dynamics of their market
7. Pioneered the use of mathematical modelling techniques to solve industrial and engineering problems.
8. Authored several papers on the use of mathematics in business and educational settings.

Born 3 October 1881, Galveston, Texas, USA. Died 15 July 1965, Cleveland, Ohio, USA.
