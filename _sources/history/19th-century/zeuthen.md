**Zeuthen** was a Danish mathematician who worked on the geometry of conic sections, algebraic surfaces and the history of mathematics.

Born 15 February 1839 Grimstrup, West Jutland, Denmark. Died 6 January 1920 Copenhagen, Denmark.
