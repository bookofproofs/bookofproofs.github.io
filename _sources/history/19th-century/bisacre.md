**Bisacre** was an engineer who was educated at Cambridge and worked for the Edinburgh publishers Blackie with whom he published a Calculus textbook. He wrote some research papers on X-ray diffraction.

Born 20 June 1885 Tonbridge, Kent, England. Died 9 November 1954 Helensburgh, Scotland.
