**Mikhail Vashchenko-Zakharchenko** worked on the theory of linear differential equations, the theory of probability and non-euclidean geometry.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Received a first-degree diploma in mathematics in 1929. 
2. Was a corresponding member and Professor of the Ukrainian Academy of Sciences. 
3. Established his own school of mathematics, focusing on "geometry, functional analysis, and topology". 
4. Administered the All-Union Mathematical Olympiad in 1973.
5. Served as chairman of the All-Union Math Committee. 
6. Was an expert reviewer in mathematics books.
7. Authored and co-authored several textbooks and monographs on differential geometry and topology. 
8. Published numerous articles in journals of mathematics, especially in the field of geometry. 
9. Developed mathematical models for non-Euclidean geometry. 
10. Represented the Soviet Union at the 196th International Congress ofMathematical Societies in 1954.

Born 12 November 1825, Maliivka, Zolotonosha, Poltava, Ukraine. Died 27 August 1912, Kiev, Russian Empire (now Kyiv, Ukraine).
