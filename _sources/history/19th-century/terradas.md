**Esteban Terradas **was Spanish was a mathematician, physicist and engineer. He held prestigious positions in Barcelona and Madrid universities and also made considerable contributions to the development of Spanish railways, aerospace, and other industries.




### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed his own theorem in geometry and differential calculus, known as the Terradas theorem.
2. He conducted a significant amount of research in the field of curves and surfaces.
3. Authored several books and papers on mathematical topics including differential equations and series.
4. Made important contributions to the theory of ordinary differential equations by introducing new and revolutionary methods.
5. Contributed to the mathematics of integral equations by establishing a novel and powerful method of solution.
6. He was a founding member of the Barcelona Academy of Sciences.
7. Developed a method of easily computing all solutions to equations in algebraic form.
