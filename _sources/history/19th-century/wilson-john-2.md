**(2)** was a Scottish mathematician who took over the Rectorship of his father's Academy in Bannockburn and later moved to Edinburgh as a tutor in mathematics and physics.

Born 21 November 1847 Montrose, Angus, Scotland. Died 8 December 1896 Edinburgh, Scotland.
