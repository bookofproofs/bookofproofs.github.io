Platon Sergeevich Poretsky was a Russian astronomer, mathematician, and logician.

Born 15 October 1846 Elisavetgrad (now Kropyvnytskyi), Ukraine. Died 22 August 1907 Joved, Chernigov guberniya, Russia.
