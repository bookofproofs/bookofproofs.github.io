Georgii Pfeiffer did important work on partial differential equations.

Born 24 October 1872 Sokyryntsi, Pryluka, Poltava gubernia, Ukraine. Died 10 September 1946 Kiev, Ukranian SSR (now Kyiv, Ukraine).
