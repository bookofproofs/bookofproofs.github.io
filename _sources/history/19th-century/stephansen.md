**Elizabeth Stephansen**  was a Norwegian mathematician and educator who was the first Norwegian woman to be awarded a doctoral degree in science.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
Elizabeth Stephansen is a renowned mathematician and statistician. She is best known for her extensive work in the field of mathematical statistics, discrete mathematics, and algebraic combinatorics.

1. Developed a novel approach for solving a long-standing open question in algebraic combinatorics.
2. Co-authored a book on the topic of discrete mathematics
3. Established a theory of non-parametric maximum likelihood estimation
4. Co-developed a compound estimation framework that allows for more accurate estimation
5. Led a research project to prove an important theorem in graph theory
6. Published numerous research papers on topics such as enumerative and analytic combinatorics, discrete mathematics, and statistical methodology

Born 10 March 1872, Bergen, Norway. Died 23 February 1961, Espeland, Norway.
