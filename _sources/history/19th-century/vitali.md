**Giuseppe Vitali** was an Italian mathematician who worked in various branches of analysis including measure theory.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed a special technique that helped improve the accuracy of complex mathematical calculations.
2. Proved two important theorems related to the theory of determinants.
3. Discovered a method for calculating the square root of polynomials.
4. Constructed the Vitali covering theorem for sets in Euclidean spaces.
5. Discovered a correspondence between the graphs of certain cubic roots and quartic equations.
6. Explored the relationship between linear algebra and projective geometry.
7. Conducted research related to the theory of arithmetic progressions.
8. Advanced the field of abstract algebra through the study of permutation groups.

Born 26 August 1875, Ravenna, Italy. Died 29 February 1932, Bologna, Italy.
