**Olds** was an American mathematician and statistician. He was mainly interested in probability and was regarded as an outstanding teacher.

Born 20 April 1898 Buffalo, Erie, New York, USA. Died 10 October 1961 Pittsburgh, Allegheny, Pennsylvania, USA.
