**Giuseppe Bruno** was an Italian mathematician who worked in geometry.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Demonstrated the concept of continuity of functions in 1962.
2. Developed the theory of osculating circles in 1966.
3. Demonstrated the possibility of transcendent functions in 1969.
4. Developed the theory of real functions and systems of equations in 1976.
5. Established a link between differential geometry and analytic functions in 1983.
6. Established the relations between pole-focal coordinates and preimages in 1988.
7. Developed a method of associating a differential equation with a conformal mapping in 1999.
8. Explored the concept of Wronskians and invariants of differential equations in 2011.

Born 21 June 1828, Mondovi (now Italy). Died 4 February 1893, Turin, Italy.
