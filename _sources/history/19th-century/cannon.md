**Annie Jump Cannon** was an American astronomer whose work led to the Harvard Stellar Classification Scheme.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed the stellar classification system that arranged stars from hottest to coolest. 
2. Developed a system for cataloging stars; cataloged over 225,000 stars.
3. Assisted in the production of the Henry Draper Catalog, which contains images and descriptions of over 10,000 stars.
4. Was the first woman to be elected to the American Astronomical Society.
5. Demonstrated a correlation between spectral type and absolute magnitude of stars.

Born 11 December 1863, Dover, Delaware, USA. Died 13 April 1941, Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA.
