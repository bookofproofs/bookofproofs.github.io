Regius Professor of Mathematics at University of St Andrews from 1921 to 1950. He was an expert in Invariant Theory and an enthusiast for the History of Mathematics.

Born 31 August 1885 Tettenhall, Wolverhampton, England. Died 4 May 1961 Grasmere, Westmoreland, England.
