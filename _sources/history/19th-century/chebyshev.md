**Pafnuty Chebyshev** is largely remembered for his investigations in number theory. Chebyshev was also interested in mechanics and is famous for the orthogonal polynomials he invented.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed the Chebyshev inequality and theorem.
2. Developed the Chebyshev integral inequality.
3. Developed the Prime Number Theorem.
4. Discovered the law of the distribution of the primes.
5. Pioneered the concept of probability distribution.
6. Pioneered the theory of Fourier series.
7. Developed mathematical models for percolation and diffusion.
8. Established criteria for the convergence of a sequence.
9. Developed methods to determine the exact error of numerical methods.
10. Established a criterion of stability for linear difference equations.

Born 16 May1821, Okatovo, Kaluga Region, Russia. Died 8 December 1894, St Petersburg, Russia.
