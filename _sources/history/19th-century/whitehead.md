**Whitehead** was a mathematician and philosopher who collaborated with Bertrand Russell on _Principia Mathematica_ (1910-13).

Born 15 February 1861 Ramsgate, Isle of Thanet, Kent, England. Died 30 December 1947 Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA.
