**Gomes Teixeira**  was a Portuguese mathematician and historian of mathematics.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Founded the Portuguese Mathematical Society in 1934 and served as its first president.
2. Published numerous mathematics research papers in prestigious international journals.
3. Developed several original mathematical theories, including the theory of groupoid algebras.
4. Authored several books, including History of Mathematics in Portugal and Portugal and Mathematics. 
5. Established a range of scholarships and awards for young mathematicians to foster research and promote the study of mathematics. 
6. Received the Commander of the Order of Aviz award for his achievements in mathematics. 
7. Elected Member of the Portuguese Academy of Sciences in 1942. 
8. Made major contributions to the theory of integral equations and the development of applications in mechanics and engineering.
