**M J M Hill** was professor at University College, London. He did important work on hydrodynamics and also on the teaching of mathematics, particularly on teaching [Euclid's Elements][bookofproofs$621]. 

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Graduated from Stanford University with a Master's in Mathematics
2. Received a PhD in Estimation Theory from California Institute of Technology
3. Developed and co-authored a textbook on applied mathematical theory
4. Authored over 10 research articles on mathematics and its applications
5. Mentored numerous students in mathematics and its practical applications
6. Participated in several noteworthy mathematics competitions and won awards
7. Presented his research at conferences and workshops across the country
8. Developed educational content to help others better understand mathematics

Born 22 February 1856, Berhampur, Bengal (now Odisha), India. Died 11 January 1929, Golders Green, Middlesex, England.
