Scholar developed new methods and algorithms for solving various mathematical problems and used computers to aid in calculating solutions resulting in groundbreaking solutions.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Discovered and developed several new methods for solving differential equations.
2. Developed an algorithm for solving systems of differences equations.
3. Established a general theory for the theory of hypergeometric functions.
4. Published a book of partial solutions of the Laplace equation in 1909.
5. Developed and improved computing techniques to speed up the computation and evaluation of mathematical formulas.
6. Pioneered the use of computers to calculate numerical solutions to differential equations.
7. Created a powerful new technique for solving integral equations.
8. His work resulted in groundbreaking new solutions to long-standing problems in mathematics.

Born 1893, Cork, Ireland. Died 4 December 1943.
