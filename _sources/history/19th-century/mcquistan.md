Dougald McQuistan studied at Glasgow and Cambridge. He became Professor of Natural Philosophy at the Royal Technical College in Glasgow.

Born 1879 Inverkip, Renfrew, Scotland. Died 2 April 1946 Burnside, Rutherglen, Lanarkshire, Scotland.
