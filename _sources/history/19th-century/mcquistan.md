**Dougald McQuistan** studied at Glasgow and Cambridge. He became Professor of Natural Philosophy at the Royal Technical College in Glasgow.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
* Dougald McQuistan was educated at Whitehill School, Glasgow, completing his secondary education there in 1896.
* Returning to Glasgow, McQuistan was appointed to the Natural Philosophy Department of the Royal Technical College from 1906 to 1912.
* McQuistan was a member of the Edinburgh Mathematical Society, joining in December 1908.

Born 1879, Inverkip, Renfrew, Scotland. Died 2 April 1946, Burnside, Rutherglen, Lanarkshire, Scotland.
