**Zhukovsky** was a Russian scientist who did pioneering work on aerodynamics. He is best known for the so-called Joukowski aerofoil.

Born 17 January 1847 Orekhovo, Vladimir gubernia, Russia. Died 17 March 1921 Moscow, USSR.
