**Bertrand** was a Paris professor who, in 1845, conjectured:I've told you once and I'll tell you again There's always a prime between n and 2n.This was proved by Chebyshev in 1850.

Born 11 March 1822 Paris, France. Died 3 April 1900 Paris, France.
