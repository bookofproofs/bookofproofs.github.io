**Neil McArthur** graduated from Glasgow University and apart from a period of war service spent his whole career at the University.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
* Neil attended Whitehill School, Glasgow, then from 1900 he studied at the University of Glasgow where he was awarded an M.A. in 1905 followed by a B.Sc. In 1906 McArthur was appointed as an Assistant in Mathematics at the University of Glasgow being promoted to Lecturer in 1908.

Born 13 June 1883, West Loch Tarbert, Argyll, Scotland. Died 28 December 1951, Glasgow, Scotland.
