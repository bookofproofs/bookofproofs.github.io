**Zaremba** was a Polish mathematician who worked in partial differential equations and classical analysis, particularly on harmonic functions.

Born 3 October 1863 Romanówka, Trembowla, Galicia, Austria-Hungary (now Ukraine). Died 23 November 1942 Kraków, Poland.
