**Żorawski** was a Polish mathematician who worked on invariants of differential forms, integral invariants of Lie groups, differential geometry and fluid mechanics.

Born 22 June 1866 Szczuczyn, Poland (now Shchuchyn, Belarus). Died 23 January 1953 Narutowicz, Poland.
