**Fraser** was an Edinburgh teacher. With Barclay and Knott he was one of the writers who originally proposed the founding of the Edinburgh Mathematical Society.

Born 3 May 1857 Cargill, near Perth, Scotland. Died 9 November 1890 Glasgow, Scotland.
