**Watt** was an Edinburgh lawyer and actuary. He was interested in science, being a member of the Scottish Meteorological Society and the Royal Scottish Geographical Society.

Born 21 March 1863 Edinburgh, Scotland. Died 3 December 1945 Edinburgh, Scotland.
