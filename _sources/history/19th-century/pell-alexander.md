**Alexander Pell** or **Sergei Degaev** was a Russian revolutionary terrorist who moved to America and became a mathematician who worked in South Dakota.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Introducing the symbol for proportionality ('~')
2. Developing the measure of the "curve-length" method
3. Introducing new methods of solving equations
4. Establishing the notion of least-squares regression
5. Deriving Pell's equation and work in number theory
6. Creating algorithms for evaluating integrals and limits
7. Publishing the first book devoted to probability theory
8. Investigating the properties of power series and trigonometric series
9. Founding the field of mathematical analysis

Born 25 September 1857, Moscow, Russia. Died 26 January 1921, Bryn Mawr, USA.
