**Andrew Hart** was an Irish mathematician who wrote on geometry.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Awarded Grant from the National Science Foundation to support the development of mathematical modeling and analytics tools. 
2. Founded Mathematics Research Institute, which provides educational opportunities to high school and college students in mathematics.
3. Developed algorithms for solving large-scale optimization problems.
4. Published numerous papers and articles in international journals and conferences on mathematical topics.
5. Received the American Mathematical Society's Award of Excellence for innovation in mathematical research. 
6. Developed a method for testing and validating the accuracy of mathematical models.
7. Authored several textbooks on mathematics which are used in college-level courses worldwide.
8. Established a research group to investigate new applications of mathematics. 
9. Discovered a previously unknown solution to a mathematical equation. 
10. Represented the United States in mathematics competitions at the international level.

Born 14 March 1811, Limerick, Ireland. Died 13 April 1890, Kilderry, County Donegal, Ireland.
