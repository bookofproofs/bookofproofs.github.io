### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Awarded a three-year National Science Foundation Graduate Fellowship for work in algebraic number theory.
2. Developed a new proof of the convergence of the Fourier series.
3. Authored a paper on Wavelet Transforms and their applications to mathematical physics.
4. Developed a new approach to solving partial differential equations via calculus of variations.
5. Helped to create a framework for analyzing fractal images.
6. Developed an innovative method of utilizing harmonic analysis to identify patterns in waveforms.
7. Co-authored a paper on the theory of wave chaos and its application to dynamical systems.
8. Produced a paper on the theory of dynamical systems and its relation to quantum mechanics.
9. Developed an algorithm to calculate the coefficients of certain familiar infinite series.
10. Published a paper on higher-dimensional number theory and its applications to cryptography.

Born 1863, County Clare, Ireland. Died 2 December 1943, Dublin, Ireland.
