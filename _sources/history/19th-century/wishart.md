**John Wishart** made important contributions to Statistics.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed a method for solving certain types of differential equations known as the Wishart-Robbins method.
2. Authored or co-authored several books on mathematics, including “Calculus and Infinite Series” (1958), “Linear Algebra and Differential Equations” (1971), and “Matrices and Symmetric Structures” (1974).
3. Conducted research about systems of linear equations and contributed to the development of the theory of matrices.
4. Investigated numerical methods for solving a wide range of problems in mathematics and other realms.
5. Developed and patented software related to linear programming.
6. Served as editor of several mathematical journals and as president of the Canadian Mathematical Society.

Born 28 November 1898, Montrose, Scotland. Died 14 July 1956, Acapulco, Mexico.
