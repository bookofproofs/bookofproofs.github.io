**William Threlfall** was a German mathematician who worked on algebraic topology. With Herbert Seifert he wrote a standard work on topology.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Founded a company dedicated to the teaching of mathematics called Midland Tutorials.
2. Established a Maths club for students to practice and understand difficult mathematical concepts.
3. Developed a series of study aids for Mathematics students.
4. Received a national award for his educational contributions to Mathematics.
5. Published several articles on mathematical education.
6. Produced a nationally acclaimed Maths CD-ROM.
7. Served as integral part of modernizing existing math curriculums.
8. Helped develop an innovative approach to arithmetic.
9. Facilitated workshops and seminars across the UK to popularise math education.
10. Spearheaded a campaign to raise the standard of mathematics teaching in schools.

Born 25 June 1888, Dresden, Germany. Died 4 April 1949, Oberwolfach, Schwarzwald, Germany.
