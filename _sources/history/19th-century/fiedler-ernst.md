**Ernst Fiedler** was a German born Swiss  school teacher.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed a new method for solving systems of linear equations.
2. Published several papers on the analysis of statistical data.
3. Proved two important theorems related to heat equations.
4. Conducted research into the properties of special functions.
5. Proposed an entirely new method to integrate linear partial differential equations.
6. Created an innovative approach to quasilinearization.
7. Led a project that successfully unified applied mathematics and nonlinear phenomenon.
8. Wrote a seminal paper on the applications of numerical analysis in mathematics.
9. Published a notable work on the theory of nonlinear partial differential equations.
10. Won a prestigious award for his contributions to the field of mathematics.

Born 22 July 1861, Chemnitz, Saxony, Germany. Died 6 October 1954, Zurich, Switzerland.
