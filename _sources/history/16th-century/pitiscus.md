**Bartholomeo Pitiscus** was a Polish theologian who first coined the word _Trigonometry_.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Authored the first published trigonometric tables (1595)
2. Helped to reform calendrical calculation (1583)
3. Introducing the significant digit notation (1584)
4. Discovered the law of cosines (1595)
5. Developed the first trigonometric methods to solve spherical triangles (1595) 
6. Wrote Mathematical jewel of Pitiscus (1588)
7. Contributed to the theory of quadratic equations
8. Advanced the study of logarithms and computation

Born 24 August 1561, Grünberg, Bohemia (now Zielona Góra, Poland). Died 2 July 1613, Heidelberg, Electoral Palatinate (now Germany).
