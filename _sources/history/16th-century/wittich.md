**Wittich** was a Polish mathematician and astronomer who anticipated Tycho Brahe's planetary theory. He may have influenced Napier.

Born 1546 Breslau, Hapsburg Empire (now Wroc≈Çaw, Poland). Died 9 January 1586 Vienna, Austria.
