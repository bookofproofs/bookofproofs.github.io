**Valerio** was an Italian mathematician who applied methods of Archimedes to find volumes and centres of gravity of solid bodies. He corresponded with Galileo.

Born 1552 Naples, Kingdom of Naples (now Italy). Died 17 January 1618 Rome, Papal States (now Italy).
