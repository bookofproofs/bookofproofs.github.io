**Biancani** was an Italian Jesuit astronomer and mathematician who made observations of the moon and planets with the newly invented telescope.

Born 8 March 1566 Bologna, Papal States (now Italy). Died 7 June 1624 Parma, Duchy of Parma and Piacenza (now Italy).
