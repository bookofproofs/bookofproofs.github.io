 500|"Yativrsabha":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/yativrsabha Yativrsabha was born about 500 in India.  505|"Varahamihira":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/varahamihira Varahamihira was born 505 in Kapitthaka, India. He discovered a version of Pascal's triangle and worked on magic squares. Varahamihira died in 587 in India./||

 598|"Brahmagupta":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/brahmagupta Brahmagupta was born 598 in (possibly) Ujjain, India. Brahmagupta was the foremost Indian mathematician of his time. Abu Mahmud Hamid ibn al-Khidr Al-Khujandi died in 1000./||
 940|"Abu Sahl Waijan ibn Rustam al-Quhi":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/abu-sahl-waijan-ibn-rustam-al-quhi Abu Sahl Waijan ibn Rustam al-Quhi was born about 940 in Tabaristan (now Mazanderan), Persia (now Iran).  940|"Vijayanandi":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/vijayanandi Vijayanandi was born about 940 in Benares (now Varanasi), India. Vijayanandi was an Indian mathematician and astronomer who made some contributions to trigonometry. Vijayanandi died about 1010 in India./||
 945|"Abu Said Ahmad ibn Muhammad Al-Sijzi":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/abu-said-ahmad-ibn-muhammad-al-sijzi Abu Said Ahmad ibn Muhammad Al-Sijzi was born about 945 in Sijistan, Persia (now Iran).  946|"Gerbert of Aurillac":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/gerbert-of-aurillac Gerbert of Aurillac was born 946 in Belliac, Auvergne, France.  950|"Abu'l-Hasan Ali ibn Abd al-Rahman ibn Yunus":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/abu'l-hasan-ali-ibn-abd-al-rahman-ibn-yunus/||
 953|"Abu Bekr ibn Muhammad ibn al-Husayn Al-Karaji":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/abu-bekr-ibn-muhammad-ibn-al-husayn-al-karaji Abu Bekr ibn Muhammad ibn al-Husayn Al-Karaji was born on 13 April 953 in Baghdad (now in Iraq). Abu Bekr ibn Muhammad ibn al-Husayn Al-Karaji died about 1029./||
 965|"Abu Ali al-Hasan ibn al-Haytham":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/abu-ali-al-hasan-ibn-al-haytham Abu Ali al-Hasan ibn al-Haytham was born 965 in (possibly) Basra, Persia (now Iraq). Abu Ali al-Hasan ibn al-Haytham died in 1040 in (possibly) Cairo, Egypt./||
 970|"Abu Nasr ibn Ali ibn Iraq Mansur":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/abu-nasr-ibn-ali-ibn-iraq-mansur-/||

 973|"Abu Arrayhan Muhammad ibn Ahmad al-Biruni":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/abu-arrayhan-muhammad-ibn-ahmad-al-biruni Abu Arrayhan Muhammad ibn Ahmad al-Biruni was born on 15 September 973 in Kath, Khwarazm (now Kara-Kalpakskaya, Uzbekistan). Abu Arrayhan Muhammad ibn Ahmad al-Biruni died on 13 December 1048 in Ghazna (now Ghazni, Afganistan)./||
 980|"Abu Mansur ibn Tahir Al-Baghdadi":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/abu-mansur-ibn-tahir-al-baghdadi Abu Mansur ibn Tahir Al-Baghdadi was born about 980 in Baghdad, Iraq.  980|"Abu Ali al-Husain ibn Abdallah ibn Sina  Avicenna":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/abu-ali-al-husain-ibn-abdallah-ibn-sina-(avicenna)/||
 989|"Abu Abd Allah Muhammad ibn Muadh Al-Jayyani":https://www.bookofproofs.org/history/abu-abd-allah-muhammad-ibn-muadh-al-jayyani Abu Abd Allah Muhammad ibn Muadh Al-Jayyani was born 989 in Cordoba, Andalusia (now Spain). Abu Abd Allah Muhammad ibn Muadh Al-Jayyani died in after 1079 in possibly Jaén, Andalusia (now Spain)./||
