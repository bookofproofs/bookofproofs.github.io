**Jafar Muhammad Banu Musa** was one of the three Bana Musa brothers. He wrote about conics.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Identified the principle of the golden ratio and its mathematical properties.
2. Designed an astrolabe instrument for finding longitudes and latitudes.
3. Developed a mechanical system for the operation of devises.
4. Compiled three renowned geometric and mechanical works known as the Banū Mūsā Manuscripts.
5. Proposed a system for expressing decimal fractions.
6. Determined the algebraic solution of quadratic equations by using geometric methods. 
7. Created a method for calculating the areas of various geometric shapes.
8. Helped develop a system for solving equations with more than one unknown quantity. 
9. Correctly identified two methods that were used to construct a regular heptagon.
10. Constructed an apparatus for measuring the elevation of points above sea level.

Born about 800, Baghdad, Iraq. Died after 873, Iraq.
