**Jean-François Champollion** founded scientific Egyptology and played a major role in the decipherment of Egyptian hieroglyphics and the demotic script.




### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Deciphered the Rosetta Stone by comparing it to the known language of Ancient Greek, making him the first to ever decipher Egyptian hieroglyphics.
2. Identified the phonetics of hieroglyphs and their corresponding alphabetic equivalents, thus laying the foundations of the modern Egyptology.
3. Developed a translation system allowing for accurate interpretation of ancient Egyptian texts, giving scholars a groundbreaking insight into the history of the civilization.
4. Wrote numerous books on Egyptology, including the multi-volume Egypte Ancienne et Egypte Moderne, a thorough review of Ancient Egypt.
5. Was the first to identify and name the 18 different varieties of hieroglyphic scripts.
6. Identified the concept of the modern calendar from ancient Egyptian texts, allowing for accurate dating of historical events.

Born 23 December 1790, Figeac, Lot, France. Died 4 March 1832, Paris, France.
