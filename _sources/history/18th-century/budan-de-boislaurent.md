**De Boislaurent** was a Haitian born amateur mathematician best remembered for his discovery of a rule which gives necessary conditions for a polynomial equation to have n real roots between two given numbers.

Born 28 September 1761 Limonade, Cap-Français, Saint-Domingue (now Haiti). Died 6 October 1840 Paris, France.
