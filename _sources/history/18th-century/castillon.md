**Johann Castillon** was an Italian mathematician and astronomer who wrote on the cardioid.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed a new proof concerning the binomial theorem.
2. Published the first translation of Diophantus' Arithmetica into Latin.
3. Used complex geometric constructions to prove complicated algebraic results.
4. Proposed an early form of Newton's binomial theorem for positive integers.
5. Completed an edition of the works of Nicomachus of Gerasa.
6. Solved a practical problem concerning the calculation of a line joining two points on a parabola.

Born 15 January 1708, Castiglione, Tuscany (now Italy). Died 11 October 1791, Berlin (now Germany).
