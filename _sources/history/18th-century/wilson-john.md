Best known for [Wilson's theorem][bookofproofs$8193]: If `$p$` is prime then `$1 + (p - 1)!$` is divisible by `$p$`.

Born 6 August 1741 Applethwaite, Westmoreland, England. Died 18 October 1793 Kendal, Westmoreland, England.
