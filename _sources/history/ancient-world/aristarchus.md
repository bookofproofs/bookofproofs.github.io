**Aristarchus Of Samos** was a Greek mathematician and astronomer who is celebrated as the exponent of a Sun-centred universe and for his pioneering attempt to determine the sizes and distances of the Sun and Moon.

Born about 310 BC Samos, Greece. Died about 230 BC Greece.
