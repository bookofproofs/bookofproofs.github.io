**Bryson Of Heraclea** was a Greek mathematician who was probably a pupil of Socrates and contributed to the problem of squaring the circle.  

Bryson claimed that the circle was greater than all inscribed polygons and less than all circumscribed polygons.

Born about 450 BC Tarentum, Heraclea (now Taranto, Italy). Died ?.
