**Hipparchus Of Rhodes** was a Greek mathematician who compiled an early example of trigonometric tables and gave methods for solving spherical triangles.

Born 190 BC Nicaea (now Iznik), Bithynia (now Turkey). Died 120 BC probably Rhodes, Greece.
