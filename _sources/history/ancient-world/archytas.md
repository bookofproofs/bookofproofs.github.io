**Archytas Of Tarentum** was a Greek mathematician, statesman and philosopher who worked on the harmonic mean and the problem of duplicating the cube.  

Finding two mean proportionals between two line segments 

A solution to the problem of duplicating the cube 

Proof, that there can be no number which is a geometric mean between two numbers in the ratio `$\frac{n+1}n.$`.

Born about 428 BC Tarentum, Magna Graecia (now Taranto, Italy). Died about 350 BC.
