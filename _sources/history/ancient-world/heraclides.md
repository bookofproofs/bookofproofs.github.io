**Heraclides Of Pontus** was a Greek astronomer who proposed that the earth rotates on its axis once a day and who may have believed that the sun was the centre of the solar system.

Born 387 BC Heraclea Pontica (now Eregli, Turkey). Died 312 BC Heraclea Pontica.
