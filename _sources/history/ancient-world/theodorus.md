**Theodorus Of Cyrene** was a Greek philosopher in the Cyrenaic school of moral philosophy. He was a pupil of Protagoras and the tutor of Plato and of Theaetetus.  

Contribution to the development of irrational numbers, Theodorus proved that `$\sqrt 3, \sqrt 5, \ldots, \sqrt {17}$` were not commensurable in length with the unit length.

Born 465 BC Cyrene (now Shahhat, Libya). Died 398 BC Cyrene (now Shahhat, Libya).
