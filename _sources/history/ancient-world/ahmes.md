**Ahmes** was the Egyptian scribe who wrote the Rhind Papyrus - one of the oldest known mathematical documents.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Compiled and recorded a set of mathematical knowledge known as the Rhind Papyrus.
2. Conducted complex calculations involving fractions and coefficients.
3. Developed a concept of calculation with unit fractions.
4. Discovered a method for correctly calculating the area of irregular shapes.
5. Developed an approximation method for calculating pi.
6. Discovered a way of constructing an equation for describing a line's slope.
7. Explored the applications of mathematics to investigate areas such as astronomy, engineering and architecture.

Born about 1680 BC, Egypt. Died about 1620 BC, Egypt.
