**Apollonius Of Perga** was a Greek mathematician known as 'The Great Geometer'. His works had a very great influence on the development of mathematics and his famous book "Conics" introduced the terms parabola, ellipse and hyperbola.

Born about 262 BC Perga, Pamphylia, Greek Ionia (now Murtina, Antalya, Turkey). Died about 190 BC Alexandria, Egypt.
