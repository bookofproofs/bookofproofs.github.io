**Posidonius Of Rhodes** was a Greek philosopher, astronomer and mathematician. He followed the Stoic doctrine and attemped to estimate the size of the Sun and moon.

Born 135 BC Apameia, near Shaizar, Syria. Died 51 BC Rhodes.
