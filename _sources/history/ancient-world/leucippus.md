**Leucippus Of Miletus** was a Greek philosopher who originated the atomic theory of indivisibles developed later by Democritus.  

Together with Democritus, joint founder of the atomic theory, i.e. the theory that matter and space are not infinitely divisible.

Born about 480 BC (possibly) Miletus, Asia Minor. Died about 420 BC.
