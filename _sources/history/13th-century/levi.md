**Levi ben Gerson** was a French scholar who wrote on trigonometry as well as astronomy and philosophy.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed the philosophic method that resolved the problems of mathematical logic.
2. Authored Philosophia, a philosophical essay for solving mathematical problems.
3. Developed a system for completing square roots.
4. Developed a method for solving equations with three unknowns.
5. Elaborated a method of approximating area under a curve using straight line segments.
6. Posed and solved mathematical problems with complex algebraic equations.
7. Published Sefer ha-Mispar, a book on arithmetic, algebra and trigonometry.
8. Developed a method of subtracting one number from another that utilized the principles of geometry. 
9. Published Sefer ha-Hibur, an introduction to combinatorial mathematics.
10. Developed an algorithm for finding the greatest common factor of two numbers that calculated the product of the factors of one number and the quotients of the other number.
