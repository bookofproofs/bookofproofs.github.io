**Youqin** was a Chinese mathematician and astronomer who published work on the structure of the universe. He described how to calculate $\pi$.

Born 26 July 1271 Poyang, now Jiangxi province, China. Died about 1335 Longyou Mountains, Zhejiang province, China.
