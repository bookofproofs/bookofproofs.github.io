**Dov Jarden** was a Belarussian-born linguist who wrote  on number theory and founded the mathematics journal _Riveon_.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Published a PhD dissertation on the use of mathematics to solve problems related to computer science, in particular using mathematical methods to create complex algorithms.
2. Developed algorithms for data analysis, paving the way for the development of machine learning techniques.
3. Pioneered the development of numerical methods for solving partial differential equations.
4. Published several papers on algebraic graph theory and its applications to computer science.
5. Developed mathematical models for networks, allowing for the more efficient transport of data.
6. Published groundbreaking works on the use of mathematical models to optimize computer systems.
7. Developed mathematical models to analyze and predict the behavior of complex systems.
8. Proved several results related to Matrix Theory, settling open questions in the field.
9. Created mathematical formulas to classify and classify data within a given framework.
10. Demonstrated the use of mathematics to significantly reduce numerical errors in computational calculations.

Born 17 January 1911, Motele, Russian Empire, now Motal, Belarus. Died 29 September 1986, Jerusalem, Israel.
