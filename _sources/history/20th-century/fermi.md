**Fermi** was an Italian physicist who created the world's first nuclear reactor. He made important contributions to the development of quantum theory, nuclear and particle physics and to statistical mechanics.

Born 29 September 1901 Rome, Italy. Died 28 November 1954 Chicago, Illinois, USA.
