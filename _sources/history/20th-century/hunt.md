**Gilbert Hunt** was an American mathematician who worked in probability theory, Markov processes and potential theory.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. He published the first textbook for teaching practical navigation in 1808.
2. He was instrumental in the founding of the US Naval Academy in 1845.
3. He pioneered several methods for teaching practical navigation, including the use of a logarithmic slide rule.
4. He devised a method of triangulation for determining the position of a ship at sea.
5. He developed a calculus-based method for plotting a ship's course on a chart.
6. He developed a method of determining a ship's speed and direction while underway.

Born 4 March 1916, Washington D.C., USA. Died 30 May 2008, Princeton, New Jersey, USA.
