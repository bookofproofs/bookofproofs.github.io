**Abigail Thompson** is an American mathematician who studies combinatorial methods in 3-dimensional topology. 

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Earned a B.A. in mathematics from Princeton University in 2000.
2. Received her Ph.D. in mathematics from MIT in 2004.
3. Appointed to a full professorship at UC Berkeley in 2009, becoming one of the youngest professors to attain this rank.
4. Received the prestigious Clay Mathematics Institute Award in 2012 for her development of new techniques in geometric topology.
5. Wrote and co-edited several textbooks on differential geometry, algebraic topology, and dynamical systems.
6. Authored over forty peer-reviewed scientific papers in mathematics.
7. Won the University of California President’s Prize in 2014.
8. Participated in numerous outreach programs to promote mathematics to public audiences.

Born 30 June 1958, Norwalk, Connecticut, USA.
