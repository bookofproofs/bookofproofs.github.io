**Lang** was a French-born mathematician who spent most of his life in the USA. He is best-known for his outstanding undergraduate text-books.

Born 19 May 1927 Saint-Germain-en-Laye, near Paris, France. Died 12 September 2005 Berkeley, California, USA.
