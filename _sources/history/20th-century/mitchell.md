**Ron Mitchell** was a Scottish applied mathematician and numerical analyst.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
* A joint Mitchell/Fairweather paper, published in 1964, was the first in a series on high order alternating direction finite difference methods for elliptic PDE's.
* It was also decided to initiate a special lecture (the A R Mitchell Lecture) to be given at this and at future conferences by an eminent numerical analyst.

Born 22 June 1921, Dundee, Scotland. Died 22 November 2007, Dundee, Scotland.
