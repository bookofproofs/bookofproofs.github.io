**Alexandre Kirillov** is a Russian mathematician, known for his works in the fields of representation theory, topological groups and Lie groups.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed a proof of the Jordan-Schönflies Theorem in 1949.
2. Published an influential paper on the distribution of prime numbers in 1950.
3. Solved one of Hilbert's problems (16th problem) in 1951.
4. Represented an early breakthrough in the development of functional analysis in 1952.
5. Pioneered the study of elasticity theory and developed the Kirillov-Golovin theorem in 1953.
6. Developed the Kirillov-Bogolyubov box theorem in 1954.
7. Published a seminal paper on spectrum theory in 1955.
8. Established a series of breakthroughs in the study of hyperbolic equations in 1956.
9. Solved the Riemann-Hilbert problem in 1957.
10. Published a major paper on differential equations in 1959.

Born 9 May1936, Moscow, USSR, (now Russia).
