Ulam solved the problem of how to initiate fusion in the hydrogen bomb. He also devised the 'Monte-Carlo method' widely used in solving mathematical problems using statistical sampling.

Born 13 April 1909 Lemberg, Austrian Empire (now Lviv, Ukraine). Died 13 May 1984 Santa Fe, New Mexico, USA.
