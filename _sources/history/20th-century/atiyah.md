Michael Atiyah works in Topology and Geometry and is best known for his work on K-theory and the Atiyah-Singer Index Theorem. He was awarded a Fields Medal in 1966.

Born 22 April 1929 London, England. Died 11 January 2019 Edinburgh, Scotland.
