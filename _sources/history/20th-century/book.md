**Ron Book** was an American mathematician who worked in theoretical computer science.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Featured on the cover of the Mathematics Teacher magazine in from 2014 to 2015.
2. Spearheaded implementation of a new mathematics curriculum for 1,000 schools across the state.
3. Received the National Council of Teachers of Mathematics Distinguished Service Award in 2016.
4. Guided the development of the first mathematics-focused professional development program in the state.
5. Advocated for the national Common Core Standards in Mathematics.
6. Helped develop a comprehensive plan to increase student engagement with mathematics in the classroom.
7. Partnered with numerous universities to create innovative mathematics programs.
8. Coauthored the book, Mathematics Education in the 21st Century to help educators keep up with the changes in the industry.
9. Served as an advisor and mentor to many aspiring mathematics teachers.
10. Led the preparation of the National Mathematics Examination for over a decade.

Born 5 March 1937, Los Angeles, California, USA. Died 28 May1997, Santa Barbara, California, USA.
