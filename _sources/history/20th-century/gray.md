Marion Gray graduated from Edinburgh University and then went to Bryn Mawr College in the USA. She completed her doctorate there and returned to posts at Edinburgh and Imperial College London. She returned to the USA and worked for AT&amp;T; for the rest of her career. The Gray graph is named after her.

Born 12 May 1939 Krefeld, Germany. Died 9 January 2012 Aachen, Germany.
