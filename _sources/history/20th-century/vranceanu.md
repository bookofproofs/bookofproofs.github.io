**Gheorghe Vrﾄハceanu** was a Romanian mathematician, best known for his work in differential geometry and topology.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developing new classes of integral equations and their solutions.
2. Pioneering work on the spectral theory of linear operators.
3. Demonstrating the fundamental role of Sturm-Liouville theory in spectral theory.
4. Founding the Romanian School of Mathematics in 1959.
5. Founding the Romanian Mathematical Society in 1968.
6. Developing a new and more efficient approach to nonlinear partial differential equations.
7. Establishing the first Romanian-language journal on mathematics in 1958.
8. Establishing an international recognition of the Romanian School of Mathematics.
9. Pioneering work in the field of elliptic operators.
10. Developing a new numerical approach to boundary value problems.

Born 30 June 1900, Valea Hogii, Vaslui, Romania. Died 27 April 1979, Bucharest, Romania.
