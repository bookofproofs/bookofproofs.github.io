**Koopmans** was a Dutch American mathematician and economist. He won the Nobel Prize in Economics for his work on the theory of the optimum allocation of resources.

Born 28 August 1910 'sGraveland, Netherlands. Died 26 February 1985 New Haven Connecticut, USA.
