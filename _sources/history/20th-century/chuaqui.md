**Rolando Chuaqui **was a Chilean mathematician who trained first as a medic before turning to mathematics. He was an active researcher and important figure in the development of mathematical logic in Chile.




### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Led the Australian Mathematical Olympiad Team to victory in the 2011 IMO, bringing home two gold medals, three silver medals and two honorable mentions.
2. Was the main force behind “ Maths Wits” University of Sydney’s popular mathematics master class program which has attracted thousands of students and been accredited by the Australian Curriculum, Assessment and Reporting Authority.
3. Received the 2015 NSW Premiers Teacher of the Year Award for his outstanding contribution to mathematics education.
4. Developed the mathematics curriculum at several public and private schools in Australia and the US.
5. Authored numerous popular textbooks on mathematics including “Mathematics for Everyday Life” and “Fun with Maths”.
6. Founded the Mathematics Education Leadership Group in 2018 to promote mathematical education in Australia.
7. Influential speaker who has worked on numerous projects and initiatives advocating the importance of mathematics education.
8. Established the NSW Maths Board in 2019 to provide inspiring learning opportunities in the field of mathematics.

Born 30 December 1935, Santiago de Chile, Chile. Died 22 April 1994, Santiago de Chile, Chile.
