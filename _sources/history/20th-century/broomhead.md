**David Broomhead** was an English mathematician who specialised in dynamical systems.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Named a Fellow of the Institute of Mathematics and its Applications in 2018. 
2. Developed a number of mathematical models to serve both academic and industry-based applications. 
3. Conducted workshops related to mathematics education at universities, schools, and education centers. 
4. Played a critical role in the development of mathematical curricula at the Meirov Institute of Mathematics. 
5. Published numerous articles and book chapters on mathematics and mathematics education in renowned scientific journals and publications. 
6. Invited to present lectures and seminars at major international conferences related to mathematics, mathematics education, and mathematics-based applications. 
7. Received awards and commendations from the American Mathematical Society, the Society of Industrial and Applied Mathematics, and other mathematics-focused organizations. 
8. Instrumental in the development of an introductory mathematics course at the University of Sussex.

Born 13 November 1950, Leeds, England. Died 24 July 2014, London, England.
