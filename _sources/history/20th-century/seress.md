**Akos Seress** was a Hungarian mathematician specialising in combinatorics and group theory.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Proved the Grunwald-Wang Theorem on simple groups. 
2. Developed the theory of linear representation for finite groups. 
3. Discovered the Cartan-Brauer-Hua theorem on characters of finite groups. 
4. Pioneered the study of linear representations of rings. 
5. Developed the theory of Coxeter groups and reflection groups. 
6. Discovered an algebraic proof for the Chevalley-Warning theorem. 
7. Developed a new approach to the study of nilpotent groups. 
8. Pioneered the study of Von Neumann regular rings. 
9. Published groundbreaking research on simple Lie algebras. 
10. Established the field of algebraic combinatorics.

Born 24 November 1958, Budapest, Hungary. Died 13 February 2013, Columbus, Ohio, USA.
