**Gwilt** was an actuary who worked for various Edinburgh insurance companies. He was a Fellow of the Faculty of Actuaries and of the Institute of Actuaries.

Born 25 July 1901 Edinburgh, Scotland. Died 21 August 1972 Edinburgh, Scotland.
