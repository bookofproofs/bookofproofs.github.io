**Ivo Babuska** is a Czech mathematician who works in America and is noted for his work on the finite element method.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed Babuska-Brezzi theorem, an important result in the finite element method of numerical solution of partial differential equations
2. Established the Finite Element Method which allows for numerical simulation of equations in multiple dimensions
3. Developed the Mathematical Theory of Adaptive Control
4. Co-invented the Composite Mesh Method for reducing computational complexity
5. Developed a practical theorem proving system
6. Contributed to the development of numerical methods for the solution of partial differential equations
7. Developed a method for discretizing a domain
8. Contributed to the development of mixed finite element algorithms
9. Developed a technique that allows for analysis of nonlinear equations in multiple dimensions
10. Advanced the understanding of the Stokes equations of fluid flow and their numerical solution

Born 22 March 1926, Prague, Czechoslovakia (now Czech Republic).
