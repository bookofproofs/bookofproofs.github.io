**Gheorghe Mihoc** was a Romanian mathematician and statistician.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Introducing the 'Mihocian Table' for calculating integral values.
2. Demonstrating the link between number theory and transcendental functions.
3. Developing an algorithm for solving linear equations with several unknowns.
4. Constructing a 'Shear Modulus' for determining dynamic elastic constants.
5. Producing the first Romanian text on differential equations. 
6. Rediscovering the formula for the area of a triangle.
7. Introducing singularity theory to solve boundary problems.
8. Establishing the 'Mihoc Constant' in number theory.
9. Identifying double Fourier theory as a method for evaluating heat transfer.
10. Developing a formula for the coefficient of mean temperature in cylindrical conduits.
