**Backus** was an American mathematician best known for the invention of FORTRAN and for the BNF notation for describing the syntax of a programming language.

Born 3 December 1924 Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA. Died 17 March 2007 Ashland, Jackson, Oregon, USA.
