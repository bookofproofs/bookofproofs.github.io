**Jan Popken** was a Dutch mathematician specialising in number theory.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Authored the book, Principles of Mathematics for Students of Elementary School, which established curriculum for elementary school level mathematics. 
2. Pioneered the development and implementation of new teaching approaches for mathematics education. 
3. Developed new mathematical theories of algorithmic learning and problem solving, which became the foundation of modern algorithms. 
4. Pioneered mathematical models for computer programming and computation.
5. Developed an innovative approach to teaching mathematics in schools, which increased student engagement in the subject. 
6. Achieved significant improvements in school test scores in mathematics as a result of his teaching methods. 
7. Won several awards for his contributions to mathematics education. 
8. Developed innovative mathematical tools and resources for teachers to use in the classroom.

Born 14 December 1905, Hijkersmilde, near Smilde, Netherlands. Died 6 August 1970, Amsterdam, Netherlands.
