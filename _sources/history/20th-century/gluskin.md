**Lazar Gluskin** was a Ukranian mathematician who worked on semigroups of linear transformations.

Born 10 March 1922 Artemovsk (now Bakhmut) Donetsk Province, Ukraine. Died 15 April 1985 Kharkov, Ukraine.
