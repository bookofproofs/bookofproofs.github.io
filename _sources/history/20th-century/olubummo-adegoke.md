**Adegoke Olubummo** was a Nigerian mathematician who was the first Nigerian professor of mathematics.

Born 19 April 1923 Orin Ekiti, Ondo State, Nigeria. Died 26 October 1992 Orin Ekiti, Ondo State, Nigeria.
