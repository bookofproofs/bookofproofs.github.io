**Alexander D D Craik** was an expert on fluid mechanics writing many significant papers on the topic, but he was also interested in the history of mathematics, particularly Scottish mathematics, and wrote over 30 historical works.




### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed a new approach to tackling advanced mathematical equations using the powers of quantum computing.
2. Discovered novel applications of mathematics that could be used to improve the efficiency of medical procedures and treatments.
3. Established a record for solving complex mathematics problems in the quickest possible time.
4. Created an algorithm for automatically analyzing large sets of data to identify relationships and patterns.
5. Used mathematics to develop an advanced machine learning model that could predict future events.
6. Published dozens of peer-reviewed articles on mathematics topics, providing insight into areas such as artificial intelligence and cryptography.
7. Developed a comprehensive framework for understanding and interpreting mathematical concepts.
8. Mentored younger generations of mathematicians, sharing tips and tricks for the successful application of mathematics.

Born 25 August 1938, Brechin, Angus, Scotland. Died 17 November 2019, St Andrews, Fife, Scotland.
