Largely responsible for the present interest in Fractal Geometry. He showed how Fractals can occur in many different places in both Mathematics and elsewhere in Nature.

Born 20 November 1924 Warsaw, Poland. Died 14 October 2010 Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA.
