**Chike Obi** was a Nigerian mathematician and politician. He was the first Nigerian to hold a doctorate in mathematics. His research was on non-linear ordinary differential equations.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. First African to be awarded the Fields Medal in Mathematics (1974) 
2. Appointed as the first Professor of Mathematics at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka (1959)
3. Established the Institute of Mathematics of the University of Nigeria, Nsukka (1960)
4. Helped to develop higher education in Africa through his founding role in the establishment of the African Mathematics Union (1975)
5. Published over 80 papers and books covering fields such as mathematical analysis, algebraic topology and differential equations
6. Ranked among one of the best mathematicians in the continent of Africa

Born 7 April 1921, Zaria, Nigeria. Died 13 March 2008, Onitsha, Anambra state, Nigeria.
