**Ito** was a Japanese mathematician who pioneered the theory of stochastic integration and stochastic differential equations. He won the Gauss prize in 2006.

Born 7 September 1915 Hokusei-cho (now Inabe, Mie Prefecture), Japan. Died 10 November 2008 Kyoto, Japan.
