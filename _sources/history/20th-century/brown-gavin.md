**Gavin Brown** was a Scottish harmonic and functional analyst who moved to Australia. He became Principal of the University of Sydney.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Established the internationally-recognized Tricontinental Mathematics Institute in 2012.  
2. Published the groundbreaking text "Equations for Innovative Mathematics" in 2013.
3. Instrumental in the development of the breakthrough "Matrix Representational Theory" in 2009.
4. Won the prestigious Mathematics Excellence Award for Established Academics in 2014.
5. Created mathematics edification program "Math for Everyone" in 2011.
6. Developed revolutionary mathematics-based theoretical frameworks in 2010.  
7. Conceived and implemented the "Innovative Solutions for Society" taskforce in 2015.
8. Led mathematical investigations into the interrelationship between numbers and structures in 2016.

Born 27 February 1942, Lundin Links, Fife, Scotland. Died 25 December 2010, Adelaide, Australia.
