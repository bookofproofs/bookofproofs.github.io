**Sunday Iyahen** was a Nigerian mathematician who worked in topological vector spaces. He became a senator in the Nigerian parliament.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Contributed to the development of the African Mathematical Union (AMU).
2. Established the Nigerian Mathematical Society in 1994.
3. Authored several mathematical textbooks.
4. Spearheaded the Mathematical and Science Terraces Initiative.
5. Developed a STEM education program for primary schools in Nigeria.
6. Produced educational material on statistics and classical mathematics.
7. Was co-chair of the International Mathematics Olympiad (IMO).
8. Led a research project on the Metacurriculum of Mathematics in 2001. 
9. Represented Nigeria in the Pan African Mathematics Olympiad (2005-2010).
10. Collaborated with the International Commission on Mathematical Instruction (ICMI).

Born 3 October 1937, Benin City, Edo State, Nigeria. Died 28 January 2018, Benin City, Edo State, Nigeria.
