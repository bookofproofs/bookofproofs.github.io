**Chukwuka Okonjo** was a Nigerian mathematician and economist who was the Eze (King) of the Obahai Royal Family of Ogwashi-Ukwu

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Established the mathematics program at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, introducing modern courses in higher mathematics to the school curriculum. 
2. Authored several mathematics textbooks for the Nigerian educational system.
3. Chaired a number of national and international conferences on mathematics, advocating for the development of mathematics in Nigeria.
4. Played a key role in the development of the National Mathematical Centre in Abuja, Nigeria.
5. Convened a seminar that led to the establishment of the African Mathematical Union, now the African Mathematical Union Commission.
6. Awarded a prestigious gold medal by the International Congress of Mathematicians in 1994 for his work in mathematics and mathematics education.

Born 21 June 1928, Ogwashi-Ukwu, Delta State, Nigeria. Died 13 September 2019, Lagos, Nigeria.
