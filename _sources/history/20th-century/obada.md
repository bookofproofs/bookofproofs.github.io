**Abdel-Shafy Obada** is an Egyptian mathematician who has made important contributions to quantum field theory.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Obtained a Doctorate in Mathematics from Cairo University in 1980.
2. Becoming a professor and chair of the Mathematics Department at Al-Azhar University in 1998. 
3. Developed a new method to solve Diophantine equations which have applications to mathematics, cryptography, and Boolean algebra.
4. Authored numerous highly regarded articles and papers in the field of mathematics.
5. Successfully led numerous international conferences and scientific workshops.
6. Collaborated with colleagues from leading academic institutions around the world.
7. Established the first Mathematics Olympiad for Secondary School Students in Egypt in 2006.
8. Published the first book devoted entirely to the art of problem-solving in mathematics in 2008.
9. Awarded the Egyptian State Award in Mathematics twice, in 1997 and 2010.

Born 20 November 1942, Sanbo, Dairout, Assiut, Egypt.
