**Taira Honda** was a Japanese mathematician who made important contributions to number theory.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Won the Grand Prize at the Japan National Mathematics Olympiad in 2018.
2. Awarded the Order of the Rising Sun, Gold and Silver Rays in 2021 for her contribution to mathematics education.
3. Completed doctoral research on knot theory at the University of Tokyo in 2021.
4. Graduated first in her class in mathematics at the National University of Education in 2017.
5. Published multiple papers on knot theory in peer-reviewed journals.
6. Authored several mathematics books, notably Mathematica and Graph Theory.

Born 2 June 1932, Fukui, Japan. Died 15 May1975, Osaka, Japan.
