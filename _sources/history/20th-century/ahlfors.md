**Ahlfors** was a Finnish mathematician who made important contributions to Complex Analysis and was one of the first recipients of a Fields Medal.

Born 18 April 1907 Helsingfors, Finland, Russian Empire (now Helsinki, Finland). Died 11 October 1996 Pittsfield, Massachusetts, USA.
