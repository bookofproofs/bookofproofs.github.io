**Gelfand** was a Ukranian mathematician who made important contributions to many areas including group theory, representation theory and functional analysis.

Born 2 September 1913 Krasnye Okny, Odessa, Ukraine. Died 5 October 2009 New Brunswick, N.J., USA.
