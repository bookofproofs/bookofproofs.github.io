**Linnik** was a Ukranian-born mathematician who worked on number theory, probability theory and mathematical statistics.

Born 21 January 1915 Belaya Tserkov, Ukraine. Died 30 June 1972 Leningrad, USSR (now St Petersburg, Russia).
