**Laurence Raymond Harper Jr** was an African American whose Ph.D. at the University of Chicago was supervised by Abraham Adrian Albert. He was a mathematics professor at the University of Minnesota for 47 years.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):

Born 10 December 1929, Atlanta, Georgia, USA. Died 9 July 2002, Atlanta, Georgia, USA.
