**Don Ornstein** is an American mathematician working in the area of ergodic theory.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Made major breakthroughs in the fields of topological dynamics and ergodic theory.
2. Co-authored the widely praised 1993 text, “Ergodic Theory, Randomness, and Dynamical Systems.” 
3. Named a fellow of the American Mathematical Society in 2012.
4. Earned a prestigious National Science Foundation fellowship in 1964.
5. Received a Guggenheim Fellowship for Mathematics and Natural Sciences in 1984.
6. Received the 2003 AMS Kleinman Prize in Applied Mathematics.
7. Served as an editor for the Annals of Mathematics and Annals of Probability journals.
8. Authored a number of influential papers published in prominent mathematics journals.

Born 30 July 1934, New York, USA.
