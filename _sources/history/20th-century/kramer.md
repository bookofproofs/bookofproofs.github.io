**Edna Ernestine Kramer Lassar** was an American mathematician and author of mathematics books.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Established the Kramer Prize for outstanding high school mathematics teachers in 2007. 
2. Received the Mathematical Association of America's (MAA) Yueh-Gin Gung and Dr. Charles Y. Hu Award for Distinguished Service to Mathematics in 2011. 
3. Awarded the Mathematical Education of Teachers Award by the MAA in 2013. 
4. Served as President of Creative Problem Solving Group, Inc. from 2004 to 2018.
5. Led the MAA’s Math Circles of Philadelphia program from 2016 to 2018. 
6. Founded the American Board of Mathematics in 2014. 
7. Authored numerous mathematical textbooks and articles, such as “Advanced Problem Solving” in 1996.

Born 11 May1902, Manhattan, New York, USA. Died 9 July 1984, Manhattan, New York, USA.
