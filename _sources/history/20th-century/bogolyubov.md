**Bogolyubov** was a Russian mathematician and theoretical physicist who made contributions to quantum field theory, to classical and quantum statistical mechanics and to the theory of dynamical systems.

Born 21 August 1909 Nizhny Novgorod, Russia. Died 13 February 1992 Moscow, Russia.
