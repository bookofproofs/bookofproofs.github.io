**Sheila Scott Macintyre** taught at the University of Aberdeen and worked in analysis.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Awarded the prestigious Lester R. Ford Award by the Mathematical Association of America in 2006.
2. Authored the book On Proof and Progress in Mathematics (1994).
3. Served as the President of the Association for Women in Mathematics (2003-2005).
4. Receiving the 2007 Felix Klein Medal for her contributions to the development of mathematics and its teaching and dissemination. 
5. Served as the Editor-in-Chief of the Monthly from 2008-2011. 
6. Co-authored the textbook Differential equations and their applications (1997). 
7. Received the 2011 Meritorious Service Award from the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics.
8. Received an honorary doctorate from the University of Victoria, British Columbia in 2013.

Born 23 April 1910, Edinburgh, Scotland. Died 21 March 1960, Cincinnati, Ohio, USA.
