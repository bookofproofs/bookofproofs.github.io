**Mischa Cotlar** was a Uruguayan mathematician who worked in the fields of harmonic analysis, ergodic theory and spectral theory.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Became the youngest ever professor of mathematics in Latin America at the age of 22. 
2. Made important contributions to functional analysis, stochastic processes, nonlinear integro-differential equations, and number theory.
3. Provided key insights into the mathematical foundations of quantum mechanics. 
4. Authored six books and written numerous articles in refereed mathematics journals. 
5. Received several prestigious awards, including the Alfredo Pettoruti Prize in Mathematics and the Alexander von Humboldt Research Award. 
6. Appointed Editor-in-Chief of the Argentinian Mathematical journal. 
7. Named a member of the National Academy of Science of Buenos Aires

Born 1 August 1913, Sarny, Ukraine. Died 16 January 2007, Buenos Aires, Argentina.
