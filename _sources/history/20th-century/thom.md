**Thom** was a French mathematician who is known for his development of catastrophe theory, a mathematical treatment of continuous action producing a discontinuous result.

Born 2 September 1923 Montbéliard, Doubs, France. Died 25 October 2002 Bures-sur-Yvette, France.
