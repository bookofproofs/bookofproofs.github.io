**Zeeman** was a British mathematician known for his work in geometric topology and singularity theory. He was responsible for founding the mathematics department at Warwick University.

Born 4 February 1925 Yokohama, Japan. Died 13 February 2016 Woodstock, Oxfordshire, England.
