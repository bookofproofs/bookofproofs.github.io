**John Aitchison** was a Scottish statistician who made contributions to distribution theory, practical Bayesian statistics, statistical diagnosis in medicine and other areas of applied and theoretical statistics.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Selected as a Fellow of the Royal Society in 1992 for his mathematical research. 
2. Received the ICTM Medal for Mathematics Education in 1996 in recognition of his service to the teaching of mathematics. 
3. Appointed as the inaugural Director of the School Mathematics Project in 1978.
4. Authored or co-authored over 80 books and educational resource materials in mathematics.
5. Designed and implemented innovative teaching strategies in mathematics.
6. Delivered talks and lectures on mathematics education to audiences worldwide. 
7. Received the Royal Society Ewing Medal in 1997 for his leadership and sustained contribution to British mathematics education.

Born 22 July 1926, East Linton, East Lothian, Scotland. Died 23 December 2016, Glasgow, Scotland.
