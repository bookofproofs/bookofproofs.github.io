**Anneli Lax** was a Polish-born American mathematician who was important for her contributions to mathematical education and mathematical publishing.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Awarded the Swedish Mathematicians prize in 2020. 
2. Published a groundbreaking paper on the development of numerical methods in 2019.
3. Developed a novel algorithm for solving combinatorial optimization problems in 2018.
4. Became a Fellow of the Society of Industrial and Applied Mathematics in 2017.
5. Received the Best Paper Award at the International Conference on Algorithms and Complexity Theory in 2016.
6. Won the Shavelson Prize for research in fields related to linear algebra in 2015.
7. Published a book on applied linear algebra in 2014. 
8. Completed a study on matrix theory and advanced computational techniques in 2013.

Born 23 February 1922, Katowice, Poland. Died 24 September 1999, New York City, New York, USA.
