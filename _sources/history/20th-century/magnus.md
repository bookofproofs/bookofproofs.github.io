**Magnus** was a German American mathematician who worked in combinatorial group theory, Lie algebras, mathematical physics, elliptic functions, and the study of tessellations.

Born 5 February 1907 Berlin, Germany. Died 15 October 1990 New Rochelle, New York, USA.
