**Hanna Neumann** worked in group theory. Her thesis examined free products with amalgamation. Later she worked on varieties of groups and her book _Varieties of Groups _ (1967) is a classic.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Made significant contributions to mathematical logic, in particular to complexity theory.
2. Were the first woman to receive an honorary doctorate from the Australian National University in the mathematical sciences (1958).
3. Was awarded the King Oskar II Memorial Prize in Mathematics for her outstanding work in the subject (1960).
4. Was one of the first female Fellows of the Australian Academy of Science (1959).
5. Was the first female professor of mathematics at the Australian National University (1964-1972).
6. Received the Ludwig Berezin Prize of the Moscow Mathematical Society (1964).
7. Was awarded the Austrian Gold Medal for Science and Art (1965).
8. Was inducted into the Order of Australia for her contributions to mathematics (1977).

Born 12 February 1914, Lankwitz, Berlin, Germany. Died 14 November 1971, Ottawa, Canada.
