**Alling** was an American mathematician who worked in a wide range of mathematics, from the theory of ordered groups to Riemann surfaces, including surreal numbers.

Born 18 June 1930 Sunderland, Tyne & Wear, England. Died 27 June 1989 Lodge Farm Lane, Settle, North Yorkshire, England.
