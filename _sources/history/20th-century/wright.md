**Wright** was a number theorist and collaborator of G. H. Hardy. He was Professor of Mathematics at Aberdeen for many years and went on to become Principal of the university.

Born 13 February 1906 Farnley, near Leeds, England. Died 2 February 2005 Reading, England.
