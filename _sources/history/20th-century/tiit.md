**Ene-Margit Tiit** is an Estonian mathematician who was the first president of the Estonian Statistical Society.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Became the first female professor of mathematics at the University of Tartu in Estonia (1959) 
2. Established an Institute for Mathematical Statistics at the University of Tartu (1966) 
3. Became a Fellow of the Estonian Academy of Sciences (1991)
4. Published numerous articles about probability theory and stochastic processes
5. Co-authored ‘Mathematical Theory of Probability’ with her husband (1969) 
6. Authored numerous books on the topics of probability, fuzzy sets, and stochastic process 
7. Developed theoretical models of stochastic processes used in efficient management of financial systems 
8. Awarded the Medal of the Order of the White Star of the Republic of Estonia (1996)

Born 22 April 1934, Tartu, Estonia.
