**Boris Khvedelidze** was a Georgian mathematician who worked in the partial differential equations, integral equations, and mathematical physics. He was appointed as a Vice-President of the re-formed Georgian Mathematical Society in 1962.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):

Born 7 November 1915. Died 27 March 1993.
