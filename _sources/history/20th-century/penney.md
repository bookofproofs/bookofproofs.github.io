**William George Penney** was a British nuclear physicist who led Britain's development of the atomic bomb.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
* At this point Penney was offered a Chair at the University of Oxford.
* From 1954 Penney served on the Board of the Atomic Energy Authority, becoming Chairman in 1964.
* In 1946 he was made O.B.E., in 1952 K.B.E. and made Baron Penney of East Hendred in 1967.

Born 24 June 1909, Gibraltar. Died 3 March 1991, East Hendred (near London), England.
