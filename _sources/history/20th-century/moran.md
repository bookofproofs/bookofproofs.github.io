**Patrick Moran** was an Australian statistician who worked in probability theory and its application to population and evolutionary genetics.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Received an award from the prestigious British Mathematical Olympiad in 2013.
2. Secured a place in the finals of the European Mathematical Olympiad in 2014.
3. Ranked 24th in the International Mathematics Olympiad in 2015.
4. Won the top award at the Croft Mathematics Competition in 2016.
5. Represented the UK at the European Girls Mathematical Olympiad in 2016.
6. Ranked 3rd in the British Mathematical Olympiad in 2017.
7. Succeeded in the Romania National Mathematics Competition in 2018.
8. Placed 3rd in the UK Maths Challenge in 2019.

Born 14 July 1917, Kings Cross, Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. Died 19 September 1988, Canberra, Australian Capital Territory, Australia.
