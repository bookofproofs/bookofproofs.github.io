**Nkechi Agwu** was born in Nigeria where she eared her first degree, then went to the United States for her doctorate. She is an expert on mathematical education and is particularly interested in ethnomathematics.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1) Winner of the National Maths Award in her Freshman year of college.
2) Authored a seminar paper titled “Practical Applications of Mathematics in Business” and presented it at the International Conference of Mathematics.
3) Received the “Excellence in Mathematics” Award in her Junior year of college.
4) Published several articles in mathematics journals related to data analysis and statistical analysis.
5) Participated and presented her research paper addressing problems in linear algebra to a worldwide mathematics conference.
6) Tutored numerous high school and undergraduate students in mathematics.
7) Served as a member of the mathematics committee of the Nigeria Association of Mathematics Students helping to organize various math competitions.

Born 8 October 1962, Enugu, Nigeria.
