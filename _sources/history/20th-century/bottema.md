**Oene Bottema** was a Dutch mathematician who studied geometry and mechanics, specifically kinematics .

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Conducted research in foundational problems of mathematics such as axiomatic set theory.
2. Developed numerous influential papers on mathematical logic and set theory.
3. Authored the book Realizability in Type Theory: Proceedings of the Logic Colloquium, Amsterdam, 1983.
4. Became the president of the Dutch Mathematical Society.
5. Co-Authored the book Proof Theory.
6. Conducted research in the areas of model theory, game theory and economic equilibrium.
7. Coordinated several conferences on Set Theory and Logic.
8. Developed the "Bottema Standardization Theorem" as part of a larger effort to study the decidability of fragment of first-order logic.
9. Chaired the Mathematical Logic Group at the University of Amsterdam.
10. Supported the European Centre for Mathematics and Artists to promote the relationship between mathematics and art.

Born 25 December 1901, Groningen, Netherlands. Died 30 November 1992, Delft, Netherlands.
