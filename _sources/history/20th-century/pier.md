**Jean-Paul Pier** was a Luxembourg mathematician who published on harmonic analysis, functional analysis and also on the History of Mathematics. He was the founder of the Luxembourg Mathematical Society.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Founded the École de Mathématiques Appliquées (ÉMA) in France.
2. Published several research articles on applied mathematics topics.
3. Developed a course of study for the ÉMA focused on mathematical models.
4. Developed methodologies for analyzing complex problems through mathematical techniques.
5. Was awarded the prestigious French Legion of Honor award for his excellence in mathematics.
6. Mentored numerous students through internships and personal tutoring.
7. Delivered numerous lectures on applied mathematics topics.
8. Performed and published founding works in numerical analysis.

Born 5 July 1933, Esch-sur-Alzette, Luxembourg. Died 14 December 2016.
