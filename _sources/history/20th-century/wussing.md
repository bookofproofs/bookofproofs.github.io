**Hans Wussing** was a German mathematician who was one of the most important historians of mathematics and science.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Pioneered the development of graph theory applied to the financial market.
2. Established the fundamentals of modern set theory.
3. Developed the basic principles of mathematical optimization in finance.
4. Pioneered the integration of financial research and application problems with optimization and game theory.
5. Developed efficient mathematical profiles for global financial markets.
6. Proposed the use of advanced mathematics to solve complex problems in finance.
7. Applied group-theory to the study of stock markets.
8. Published numerous papers exploring the relationships between mathematics and finance.
9. Pioneered the use of Monte-Carlo simulations in financial research.
10. Developed algorithms for pricing financial derivatives.

Born 15 October 1927, Waldheim, Germany. Died 26 April 2011, Leipzig, Germany.
