**Sixto Ríos García** was a Spanish mathematician who wrote many important papers in both analysis and statistics.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed a revolutionary problem-solving methodology combining Number Theory and Geometry.
2. Worked to develop an efficient method for solving third-degree polynomial equations.
3. Discovered several new theorems and formulas that were previously unknown in mathematics.
4. Formulated several novel conceptual models to explain the productivity of their work.
5. Proposed a new system for calculating the highest common factor.
6. Developed a system for simplifying the use of radicals in equations.
7. Used mathematical principles to prove the existence of an underlying concept of order in the universe.
8. Developed more efficient algorithms for solving mathematical equations.
9. Constructed several complex mathematical models of astronomical phenomena.
10. Discovered new methods of deducing mathematical concepts from physical phenomena.

Born 4 January 1913, Pelahustán, Toledo, Spain. Died 8 July 2008, Madrid, Spain.
