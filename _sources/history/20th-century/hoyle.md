**Hoyle** was an English astronomer best known for his espousal of the "steady-state" theory of the universe and rejecting the "big bang" theory. He also wrote science fiction.

Born 24 June 1915 Bingley, Yorkshire, England. Died 20 August 2001 Bournemouth, England.
