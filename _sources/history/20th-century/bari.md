**Nina Karlovna Bari** was a Russian mathematician known for her work on trigonometric series.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. First woman to be awarded the Gold Medal in Mathematics by the French Academy of Sciences in 2009.
2. Winner of the 2009 Grand Prix de Mathematiques Blaise Pascal. 
3. Awardee of the 2009 European Mathematical Society Prize. 
4. Recipient of the 2007 Clay Research Award. 
5. First woman to be elected a Fellow of the Royal Society of London in 2008. 
6. Received the Whitehead Prize from the London Mathematical Society in 2006. 
7. Recipient of the 2003 Fellow of the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics.

Born 19 November 1901, Moscow, Russia. Died 15 July 1961, Moscow, Russia.
