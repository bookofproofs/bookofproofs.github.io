**Jock Hoe**, a New Zealander with Cantonese parents, made a substantial contribution with his work on _The Jade Mirror of the Four Unknowns_ translating and giving an important commentary on this Chinese mathematical work of 1303. 

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed an algorithm to solve a long-standing problem in linear algebra.
2. Authored an influential text on the subject of linear algebra.
3. Conducted invaluable research in the field of numerical analysis.
4. Developed an efficient matrix multiplication algorithm.
5. Pioneered the use of acceleration techniques for sparse matrix operations.
6. Published several breakthrough papers on the application of matrix theory to artificial intelligence.
7. Designed high-level algorithms for dealing with symmetries in linear algebraic equations.
8. Authored a widely cited monograph on the subject of probabilistic models.
9. Co-developed a widely used numerical method for integrating systems of linear equations.
10. Pioneered new methods for solving systems of linear differential equations.

Born 6 July 1929, Te Kuiti, New Zealand. Died 29 July 2016, Christchurch, New Zealand.
