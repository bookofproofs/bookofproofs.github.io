**Hoe** was a New Zealander with Cantonese parents, made a substantial contribution with his work on The Jade Mirror of the Four Unknowns translating and giving an important commentary on this Chinese mathematical work of 1303.

Born 6 July 1929 Te Kuiti, New Zealand. Died 29 July 2016 Christchurch, New Zealand.
