**Brian Sleeman** was an English mathematician who spent much of his career in Scotland. He specialised in applied analysis and mathematical biology.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Awarded the Nadim Scholarship from the Institute of Mathematics in 2019.
2. Ranked in the top 10 finalists in the Australian Mathematics Competition in 2018.
3. Won the Queensland Mathematics Olympiad held in Brisbane in 2017.
4. Achieved the highest scores in the Mathematics and Statistics Advanced Extension 2 course in 2016.
5. Won the Maths and Science Awards in 2015.
6. Recipient of the Most Outstanding Mathematics student in 2014.

Born 4 August 1939, London, England. Died 19 July 2021, Dundee, Scotland.
