Anatolii Asirovich Goldbergwas a Ukranian mathematician who moved to Israel. He worked in complex analysis.

Born 2 April 1930 Kiev, Ukranian SSR (now Kyiv, Ukraine). Died 11 October 2008 Netanya, Israel.
