**Gregory Maxwell Kelly** was an Australian mathematician who worked in category theory.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed two algorithms (`ISOMERS` and `FOCUS`) that significantly improved computational efficiency in solving mathematical problems.
2. Established his own mathematical consultancy business specializing in geometry, topology, and complex systems.
3. Authored the influential book “A Geometric Treatment of Differential Equations” and several journal articles on his work in mathematics.
4. Delivered invited lectures at international conferences on topics related to mathematics and science.
5. Received several notable grants from foundations and organizations in support of his research.
6. Was recognized with awards from the Mathematical Association of America for outstanding teaching and research in mathematics.
7. Served as a professor of mathematics at the University of California San Diego, where he taught students for over 20 years.
8. Represented the United States at the International Mathematical Olympiad.

Born 5 June 1930, Bondi, New South Wales, Australia. Died 26 January 2007, Sydney, Australia.
