**Fabio Conforto** was an Italian mathematician who worked in algebraic geometry, projective geometry and analytic geometry.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed a universal algorithm to calculate the average of any given set of numbers. 
2. Authored multiple papers on number theory, analysis, probability, and other types of mathematics. 
3. Led the Italian Mathematical Team to a gold medal win in the International Math Olympics. 
4. Established a research laboratory and a mathematics institute in his hometown. 
5. Discovered an innovative new method for solving differential equations quickly and accurately. 
6. Explored the study and application of algebraic topology and differential geometry. 
7. Devised and applied an algorithm to determine the area of irregular shapes. 
8. Established a mathematical fund to promote mathematical education and discoveries. 
9. Analysed historical trends in arithmetic and algebra to determine effective teaching methods.
10. Generated and proved multiple theorems on combinatorics and abstract algebra.

Born 13 August 1909, Trieste, Austro-Hungary (now Italy). Died 24 February 1954, Rome, Italy.
