**Hau** was a Danish physicist and mathematician. She has led a team at Harvard University who have slowed light and in 2001 succeeded in stopping a beam of light. This has important applications to quantum computing.

Born 24 November 1958 Budapest, Hungary. Died 13 February 2013 Columbus, Ohio, USA.
