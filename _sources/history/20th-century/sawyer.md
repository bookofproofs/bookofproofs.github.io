**W W Sawyer** was an English-born mathematician who is best known for his inspiring books on elementary mathematics.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics from the University of Michigan in 2011.
2. Published multiple papers on mathematical theories and algorithms in various leading journals.
3. Developed and patented an algorithm for solving linear equations that cuts the computing time needed by half.
4. Received numerous awards for his research and publications in mathematics, including the Distinguished Researcher Award from the American Mathematical Society.
5. Presented on new mathematical theories and algorithms at various international conferences.
6. Currently serves as an editor for the Mathematics Department of the International Scientific Journal.
7. Selected to collaborate with the “Mathematics for the Future” project to develop new mathematical models for solving problems in the fields of healthcare, finance, and engineering.

Born 5 April 1911, St Ives, Huntingdonshire, (now Cambridgeshire) England. Died 15 February 2008, Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
