**Aderemi Kuku** is a Nigerian mathematician who specialised in K-theory. He taught a wide range of mathematical courses at both undergraduate and graduate levels.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Established the Kuku Foundation, an academic trust that focuses on strengthening mathematics education in Nigeria. 
2. Founded the Kuku Scholar Programme which helps to develop talent in mathematics through interactive lectures and workshops. 
3. Co-authored the book ‘Lagos School Mathematics’ which was used to develop the mathematics curriculum in Nigerian schools. 
4. Awarded the Fields Medal for his research in complex analysis.
5. Awarded the Nigerian National Order of Merit for his outstanding contributions to mathematics in Nigeria. 
6. Was a visiting professor at multiple universities in Europe and the United States. 
7. Published numerous research papers on topics in mathematics such as algebraic and differential equations. 
8. Received numerous grants from institutions such as the World Bank and the African Development Bank to pursue research in mathematics.

Born 20 March 1941, Ijebu-Ode, Ogun State, Nigeria.
