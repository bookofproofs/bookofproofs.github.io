**Catherine Steele** graduated from the University of St Andrews. She went on to a doctorate in Chemistry and studied at the University of Illinois and at Harvard. She taught for some time at a Horticultural College in Kent before returning to the USA.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Created an eight-point plan for math teachers to strengthen their teaching methods.
2. Developed and implemented a math curriculum designed specifically to promote creative thinking.
3. Published a book, Spotlight on Math: A Guide to Inquiry-Based Math, based on her research and teaching experiences.
4. Presented at numerous education conferences including the NCTM Annual Meeting and Exposition. 
5. Was the recipient of multiple awards for excellence in mathematics teaching and curriculum development. 
6. Received a grant from The National Science Foundation to fund her research into the effectiveness of student-centric mathematics teaching methods.
