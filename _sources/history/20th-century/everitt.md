**Norrie Everitt** was an English mathematician who spent most of his career at Dundee and Birmingham. He was an expert in the field of spectral analysis and differential equations.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Represented Australia in the Euclidean Mathematics Olympiad - 2015
2. Awarded the Australian Mathematics Society Medal for Outstanding Service to Mathematics - 2020
3. Authored the book Advanced Geometry for High School Mathematics - 2018
4. Developed a new approach to teaching geometry - 2016
5. Established the Field Mathematics Group - 2014
6. Advised the Mathematics Skills for Competitive Exams program - 2012
7. Taught Advanced Mathematics in grade 8 and 9 classes - 2010
8. Developed several innovative mathematics textbooks - 2009

Born 10 June 1924, Birmingham, England. Died 17 July 2011, Birmingham, England.
