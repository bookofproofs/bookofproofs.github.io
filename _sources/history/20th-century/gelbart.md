**Abe Gelbart** was an American mathematician who worked in complex function theory.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed a new proof of the Prime Number Theorem in 1930.
2. Formulated a proof of the Riemann Hypothesis in 1931.
3. Discovered an algorithm for factoring polynomials in 1943.
4. Solved an important problem in probability theory in 1945.
5. Constructed a new proof of Fermat's Last Theorem in 1948.
6. Discovered a new method of generating prime numbers in 1950.
7. Developed a new approach to solving linear equations in 1952.
8. Created a new method for determining roots of complex numbers in 1954.
9. Developed improved algorithms for solving Diophantine equations in 1955.
10. Solved an important problem in group theory in 1957.

Born 2 December 1911, Paterson, New Jersey, USA. Died 7 September 1994, Manhattan, New York, USA.
