**Wigner** was a Hungarian-American theoretical physicist and mathematician who won a Nobel prize for his contributions to the theory of the atomic nucleus and elementary particles.

Born 17 November 1902 Budapest, Hungary. Died 1 January 1995 Princeton, New Jersey, USA.
