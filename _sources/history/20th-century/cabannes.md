**Henri Cabannes** was a French mathematician who worked on the mechanics of fluids and vibrating ropes.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed the theory of homological algebra.
2. Researched group cohomology, sheaf theory and intersection theory.
3. Pioneered the use of algebraic topology and homology groups in algebraic geometry.
4. Developed the theory of germs of holomorphic functions.
5. Established the analytification of algebraic varieties.
6. Spearheaded the creation of topos theory.
7. Established the cohomology of sheaves.
8. Developed important results in Hodge theory.
9. Provided key understanding in the category of schemes.
10. Pioneered the use of algebraic differentials in algebraic geometry.
