**Faddeev** was a Russian mathematician who worked mainly in algebra, and who made significant contributions to other areas such as number theory, function theory, geometry and probability.

Born 30 June 1907 Yukhnov, Smolensk, now Kaluga, Russia. Died 30 October 1989 Leningrad, USSR (now St Petersburg, Russia).
