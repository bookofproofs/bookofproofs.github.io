Paul Cohen was an American mathematian who used a technique called forcing  to prove the independence in set theory of the axiom of choice and of the generalised continuum hypothesis.

Born 2 April 1934 Long Branch, New Jersey, USA. Died 23 March 2007 Palo Alto, California, USA.
