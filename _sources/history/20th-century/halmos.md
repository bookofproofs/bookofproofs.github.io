**Halmos** was a Hungarian-American mathematician who made fundamental advances in the areas of logic, probability, statistics, operator theory and functional analysis. He is best known for some of his text-books and for his collection of mathematicians' photographs.

Born 3 March 1916 Budapest, Hungary. Died 2 October 2006 Los Gatos, California, USA.
