**Taniyama** was a Japanese mathematician best known for the Tanayama-Shimura conjecture, whose proof led to a proof of <a href="https://mathshistory.st-andrews.ac.uk/Biographies/Fermat/">Fermat</a>'s Last Theorem.

Born 12 November 1927 Kisai (north of Tokyo), Japan. Died 17 November 1958 Tokyo, Japan.
