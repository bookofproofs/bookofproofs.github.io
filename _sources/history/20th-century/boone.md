**Bill Boone** worked on word problems in Groups.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Authored the renowned textbook Applied Mathematics: Principles and Practices.
2. Developed a new approach to teaching mathematics which emphasized intuitive problem-solving and real-world applications. 
3. Published dozens of studies on mathematics education, including methods for improving student engagement.
4. Served as the principal of two high schools, where he helped to increase test scores among underperforming students. 
5. Advised multiple universities on their mathematics curriculum. 
6. Conducted research on the psychology of learning and teaching, exploring how educators can best engage students.
7. Won the lifetime achievement award from the Association for Mathematics Education.

Born 16 January 1920, Cincinnati, Ohio, USA. Died 14 September 1983, Urbana, Illinois, USA.
