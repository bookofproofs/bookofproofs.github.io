**John Smith** graduated from Glasgow University and then stayed on as a lecturer. He taught at Campbeltown Grammar School and Dollar Academy and then became an HM Schools Inspector.

Born 1905 Kilmarnock, Scotland. Died 2 August 1962 Lincoln, England.
