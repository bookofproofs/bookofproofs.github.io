**Olga Oleinik** was a Ukrainian mathematician who worked on the theory of partial differential equations.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed new approaches to problem solving in mathematical analysis.
2. Completed a dissertation on the subject of “Almost Periodic Functions of a Real Argument”.
3. Issued numerous scientific papers covering topics such as the convergence of Fourier series and the theory of functions of a real argument.
4. Published the book “Asymptotic Expansions of Analytic Functions” in 1965.
5. Served as a professor of mathematics at Moscow State University for more than 40 years.
6. Established Chair of Mathematics at the Moscow Aviation Institute in 1972.
7. Authored several books related to mathematics and its applications, including “The Mathematics of Computing” in 1987.
8. Was awarded the Hero of Socialist Labour medal in 1991.

Born 2 July 1925, Kiev, Ukranian SSR (now Kyiv, Ukraine). Died 13 October 2001, Moscow, Russia.
