Lê Văn Thiêm was a Vietnamese mathematician who became one of the founders of higher education in his country.

Born 29 March 1918 Duc Tho District, Ha Tinh Province, Vietnam. Died 3 July 1991 Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
