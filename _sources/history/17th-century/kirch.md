**Maria Winckelmann** was a German astronomer who helped her husband with his observations. She was the first woman to discover a comet.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed a solution to Fermat's Last Theorem in 1752.
2. Wrote the influential book Teoria della Verità della Matematica in 1758.
3. Improved the accuracy of trigonometric tables.
4. Constructed the first successful example of a non-constant spiral.
5. Established a classification system for linear equations.
6. Developed a formula for determining the arc of a circle.
7. Defined the concept of a centroid in geometry.
8. Made groundbreaking contributions to the field of theoretical mechanics.
9. Introduced the principles of least squares to solve complicated equations.

Born 25 February 1670, Panitzsch, near Leipzig, Germany. Died 29 December 1720, Berlin, Germany.
