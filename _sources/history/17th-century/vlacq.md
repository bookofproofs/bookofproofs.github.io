**Adriaan Vlacq** was a Dutch bookseller and publisher who devised and published tables of logarithms.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Published the first decimal tables of logarithms in 1628.
2. Developed and published the first table of natural sines in 1633.
3. Was the first to introduce and use the so-called "false position" method of solving equations.
4. Developed and published the first table of square roots in 1637.
5. Published the first tables of compound interest and decimal fractions.
6. Was the first to use, in practical calculations, the Binomial Theorem before the publication of Isaac Newtonâ€™s version.
7. Published the first table of integrals and the first numerical tables of trigonometric functions.

Born 1600, Gouda, Netherlands. Died 1667, The Hague, Netherlands.
