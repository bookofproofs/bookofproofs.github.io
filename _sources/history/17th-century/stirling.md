**Stirling** was a Scottish mathematician whose most important work Methodus Differentialis in 1730 is a treatise on infinite series, summation, interpolation and quadrature.

Born May 1692 Garden (near Stirling), Scotland. Died 5 December 1770 Edinburgh, Scotland.
