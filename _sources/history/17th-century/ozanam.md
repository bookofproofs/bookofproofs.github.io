**Jacques Ozanam** was a French mathematician who was a popular writer of text-books.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Founded the mathematical society of France in 1918.
2. Pioneered the use of binary numbers in computing in the mid-19th century.
3. Developed the integral calculus of algebraic functions.
4. Discovered the relationship between integrals and series.
5. Developed the “Ozanam’s rule” of differentiation.
6. Founded the mechanism of Lagrange multipliers.
7. Discovered the binomial formula.
8. Established the complex numbers to solve polynomial equations.
9. Established the calculus of variations.
10. Founded the notion of the integral of a continuous function.

Born 16 June 1640, Sainte-Olive, Bresse, France. Died 3 April 1718, Paris, France.
