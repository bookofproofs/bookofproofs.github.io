**János Apáczai** was born in Transylvania and wrote the Hungarian Encyclopaedia.

Born 10 June 1625 Apáca, Transylvania, Hungary (now Romania). Died 31 December 1659 Kolozsvár, Hungary (now Cluj, Romania).
