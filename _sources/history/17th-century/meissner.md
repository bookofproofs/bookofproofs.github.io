**Heinrich Meissner** was a German mathematician who wrote some popular textbooks. He was one of the founders of the Hamburg Mathematical Society.

Born 20 April 1644 Free Imperial City of Hamburg (now Germany). Died 1 September 1716 Free Imperial City of Hamburg (now Germany).
