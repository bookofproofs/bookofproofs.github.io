**Angeli** was an Italian Italian mathematician who worked on infinitesimals and used them to study spirals, parabolas and hyperbolas.

Born 21 September 1623 Venice, Venetian States (now Italy). Died 11 October 1697 Venice, Venetian States (now Italy).
