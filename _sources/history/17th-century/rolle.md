**Michel Rolle** was a French mathematician best known for the so-called [Rolle's theorem][bookofproofs$6778].

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
1. Developed important theorems in algebra, such as Rolle's Theorem and the Mean Value Theorem.
2. Established the laws of exponents in algebra.
3. Proved the fundamental theorem of algebra.
4. Demonstrated the law of trigonometric ratios.
5. Created a method for finding tangents of curves.
6. Investigated the arithmetic of bracketed numbers.
7. Established a mathematical link between calculus and algebra.
8. Developed a creative approach to solving problems in geometry.
9. Derived Euclid's theorems on parallel lines.
10. Discovered a seminal result about the sum of integral powers.

Born 21 April 1652, Ambert, Basse-Auvergne, France. Died 8 November 1719, Paris, France.
