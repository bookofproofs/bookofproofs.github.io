**Heron Of Alexandria** was an important geometer and worker in mechanics who invented many machines including a steam turbine. His best known mathematical work is the formula for the area of a triangle in terms of the lengths of its sides.

Born about AD 10 (possibly) Alexandria, Egypt. Died about AD 75.
