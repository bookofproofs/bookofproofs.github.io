**Diadochus** was a Greek philosopher who became head of Plato's Academy and is important mathematically for his commentaries on the work of other mathematicians.

Born about 411 Constantinople (now Istanbul), Byzantium (now Turkey). Died 17 April 485 Athens, Greece.
