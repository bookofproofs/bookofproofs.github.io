**Liu Hong** was a Chinese astronomer who was involved in calendar reform.

### Mathematical Profile (Excerpt):
* Liu Hong became interested in astronomy as a young boy.
* We do know that Liu presented these works to the Emperor in 174 or 175, and we also know that they were based on Buddhist beliefs.
* Perhaps the greatest of Liu's achievements was his work which led to a new calendar.

Born 129, Lo-yang,(now Luoyang), China. Died 210, China.
