**Simplicius** was a Greek mathematician who wrote commentaries on the works of other philosophers and mathematicians including Euclid and <a href="https://mathshistory.st-andrews.ac.uk/Biographies/Aristotle/">Aristotle</a>.

Born about 490 Cilicia, Anatolia (now Turkey). Died about 560 probably Athens, Greece.
